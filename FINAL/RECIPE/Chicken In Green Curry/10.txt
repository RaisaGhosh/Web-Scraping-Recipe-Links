## Ingredients for Chicken Green Curry Recipe

  * 175 gram chicken breasts
  * 1/4 tablespoon groundnut oil
  * 1/2 tablespoon Thai Green Curry Paste
  * 1/4  garlic clove
  * 100 milliliter coconut milk from a tin
  * 30 milliliter chicken stock
  * 1/2  lemongrass stalks chopped
  * 1/4  juice of lime
  * 1/2  zest of limes
  * 50 gram sugarsnap peas or green beans
  * 37.50 gram Beansprouts
  * As required handful of fresh coriander to serve
  * 1/4 teaspoon Thai fish sauce
