## What is Chow mein and its Types?

\- Advertisement -

_**Chow mein**_ are Chinese stir-fried noodles with vegetables and sometimes
meat or tofu; the name is a romanization of the Taishanese _chāu-mèn_.

The dish is popular throughout the Chinese diaspora and appears on the menus
of most Chinese restaurants abroad. It is particularly popular in India, the
UK, Nepal and the US.

\- Advertisement -

In general, the term chowmein refers to fried noodles. There are mainly two
types of Chow mein you can enjoy.

  * **Crisp chow mein:** When making crisp chow mein, the noodles are pressed flat while frying, creating a pancake-like dish. Any additional ingredients and sauces are layered on top of the noodle pancake.
  * **Steamed chow mein:** In steamed chowmein, the noodles are initially boiled in water, then stir-fried while being tossed with additional ingredients like vegetables or meat and coated in a light sauce.

Now you know about chow mein very well and this is the time to know the indian
style veg Chow men recipe.

## Veg Chowmein Recipe | how to make Street Style Chowmein at home | Indian
Chowmein Recipe

today you will get to see the recipe of making street-style chowmein so that
you will be able to make chowmein like a street at home.

Prep Time15 mins

Active Time20 mins

Course: Breakfast

Cuisine: Indian

Keyword: chowmein recipe, How to make chow mein

Yield: 4 people

### Materials

  * 10 cup Water 
  * 1 tsp Salt 
  * 1 pack  Hakka Noodles 
  * 2 tbsp  Oil 
  * 10 cloves  Chopped Garlic
  * 1 inch Chopped Ginger
  * 3 Slit Green Chills
  * 1 Sliced Onion 
  * 1 cup Cabbage 
  * 1/2 cup  Sliced Carrot
  * 1/2 cup  Sliced Capsicum 
  * Spring Onion
  * 1 tbsp  Red Chili Sauce
  * 1 tbsp  Green Chili Sauce
  * 1 tsp  Soya Sauce 
  * 1 tbsp  Tomato Ketchup 
  * 1/2 tbsp  Salt 
  * 1/2 tsp  Pepper Powder 

### Instructions

  * To make amazing street style Chowmein Noodle, first place a pan on flame and add 1.5-litre water to it and boil.

  * After bubbles appear, add 1 tsp of salt, 1 pack of Hakka noodles, and boil until 80% for 2-3 minutes.

  * When the noodles are separated, turn off the flame and drain the water.

  * After draining the hot water, wash it with normal water to stop overcooking.

  * When all the water is drained from the noodles, add 1 tsp of oil and toss the noodles well.

  * Now, place a pan on flame, add 2 tbsp of oil, and heat it on high flame.

  * After the oil is hot, add 10 chopped garlic cloves, 1-inch grated ginger, 3 slits green chilli, and saute well.

  * After some time, add 1 sliced onion and saute well on high flame until onion turns translucent.

  * Now add 1 cup of chopped cabbage, 1/2 sliced carrot, 1.2 cups sliced capsicum, spring onion greens, mix well, and cook for 1 minute.

  * After 1 minute, add 1 tbsp of red chilli sauce, 1 tbsp of green chilli sauce, 1 tsp of soya sauce, 1 tbsp of tomato ketchup and mix everything well.

  * Now add 1 tsp of salt, 1/2 tsp of pepper powder and mix well.

  * After 1 minute, add boiled noodles into the pan, mix well, and toss cook for 2 minutes.

  * After 2 minutes, turn off the flame and garnish it with spring onion greens.

  * Now your Veg Chow mein is completely ready, and you can enjoy it.

## Street Style Chowmein Recipe - Step By Step

1 - To make an amazing street style Chowmein Noodle, first place a pan on
flame and add 1.5-litre water to it and boil.

  
2 - After bubbles appear, add 1 tsp of salt, 1 pack of Hakka noodles, and boil
until 80% for 2-3 minutes.

  
3 - When the noodles are separated, turn off the flame and drain the water.  
4 - After draining the hot water, wash it with normal water to stop
overcooking.

  
5 - When all the water is drained from the noodles, add 1 tsp of oil and toss
the noodles well.  
6 - Now, place a pan on flame, add 2 tbsp of oil, and heat it on high flame.

  
7 - After the oil is hot, add 10 chopped garlic cloves, 1-inch grated ginger,
3 slits green chilli, and saute well.  
8 - After some time, add 1 sliced onion and saute well on high flame until the
onion turns translucent.

  
9 - Now add 1 cup of chopped cabbage, 1/2 sliced carrot, 1.2 cups sliced
capsicum, spring onion greens, mix well, and cook for 1 minute.

  
10 - After 1 minute, add 1 tbsp of red chilli sauce, 1 tbsp of green chilli
sauce, 1 tsp of soya sauce, 1 tbsp of tomato ketchup and mix everything well.

  
11 - Now add 1 tsp of salt, 1/2 tsp of pepper powder and mix well.  
12 - After 1 minute, add boiled noodles into the pan, mix well, and toss cook
for 2 minutes.

  
13 - After 2 minutes, turn off the flame and garnish it with spring onion
greens.  
14 - Now your Veg Chow mein is completely ready, and you can enjoy it.
