# Chicken Chopsuey Recipe

  * Niru Gupta

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Chicken Chopsuey

 **About Chicken Chopsuey Recipe** : An all time favorite Chinese side dish,
here's the recipe of the sweet and tangy chopsuey with chicken and crunchy
fried noodles.

  * Total Cook Time 50 mins 

  * Prep Time 15 mins 

  * Cook Time 35 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Chicken Chopsuey**

  * 1 1/2 Cups chicken (cooked and shredded thinly)
  * 1/2 cup capsicums, shredded
  * 1/2 cup onions, sliced
  * 2 tsp garlic, finely chopped
  * 2 tbsp oil
  * 200 gms noodles (drained well), boiled
  * 2 tbsp vinegar
  * 1/2 tsp soya sauce
  * 1 tsp sugar
  * to taste salt
  * 2 tbsp corn flour
  * Oil (to deep-fry)

## How to Make Chicken Chopsuey

HideShow Media

1.

Mix together the vinegar, soya sauce, sugar, salt and corn flour and add
enough water to make up to a cup.

2.

Keep aside.

3.

Heat the 2 tbsp oil and add the onions and garlic.

4.

Saute till a little soft.

5.

Add chicken and capsicum and stir-fry over high heat for a minute.

6.

Add the sauce ingredients and bring to a boil again.

7.

Deep fry the noodles over high heat till brown.

8.

Drain.

9.

Transfer noodles to a serving dish, pour the chicken mixture over and serve.

Key Ingredients: chicken (cooked and shredded thinly), capsicums, onions,
garlic, oil, noodles (drained well), vinegar, soya sauce, sugar, salt, corn
flour, Oil (to deep-fry)
