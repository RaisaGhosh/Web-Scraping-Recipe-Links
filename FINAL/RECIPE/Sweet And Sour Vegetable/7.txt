# Sweet and sour vegetable stir-fry

#### 18 Ingredients

  * 1 1/2 tbsp peanut oil 

  * 1 garlic clove, crushed 

  * 1 brown onion, halved, sliced 

  * 1 carrot, sliced diagonally 

  * 1/2 red capsicum, cut into 2cm pieces 

  * 100g button mushrooms, sliced 

  * 400g can baby corn spears, drained, halved 

  * 2 green onions, cut into 4cm lengths 

  * Steamed white long-grain rice, to serve 

  * ### Sweet and sour sauce

  * 1 tbsp cornflour 

  * 1/2 cup white sugar 

  * 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar 

  * 1/4 cup apple juice 

  * 1/4 cup tomato sauce 

  * 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce 

  * 1 tsp soy sauce 

  * 1 tsp oyster sauce 

  * 1/4 tsp salt

#### 3 Method Steps

  * Step 1

Make sweet and sour sauce. Blend cornflour with 2 tablespoons water in a bowl
until smooth. Add sugar, vinegar, apple juice, tomato sauce, Worcestershire
sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce and salt. Stir well to combine. Set aside.

  * Step 2

Heat a wok over high heat. Add oil. Swirl to coat. Add garlic and brown onion.
Stir-fry for 1 minute. Add carrot and capsicum. Stir-fry for 1 minute. Add
mushroom and corn. Stir-fry for 1 minute.

  * Step 3

Stir the sauce mixture and add to the wok. Stir-fry for 1 minute 30 seconds or
until sauce thickens. Toss through green onion. Serve stir-fry with rice.
