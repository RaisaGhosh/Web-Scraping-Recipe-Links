# About Chilli Potato Recipe:

Chilli potato is a spicy indo chinese style starter made with fried potatoes
tossed in chilli sauce. This chilli potato recipe will help you to make spicy,
slightly sweet & tangy restaurant style starter. The post shares making both
gravy & dry versions.
