# Honey Chilli Potato Recipe

  * NDTV Food 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How To Make Honey Chilli PotatoNDTV Beeps

 **Honey Chilli Potato Recipe** : The amazing starter recipe, honey chilli
potato can be added to any party food menu. This Chinese dish is generally
liked by people of all ages. Made with fried potatoes, honey and chilli sauce,
this snack is crunchy, flavorful and amazingly delicious. Enjoy the crispy,
spicy and tangy taste

  * Total Cook Time 35 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 25 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Honey Chilli Potato**

  *  **For Frying Potatoes:**
  * 2 nos Potatoes, sliced
  * 5 tbsp Corn flour
  * 1 1/2 tbsp Salt
  * 2 tsp Chilli Powder
  *  **For Honey Chilli Sauce:**
  * 1 1/2 tsp Garlic
  * 1 tsp Ginger
  * 2 nos Whole Red Chillies , chopped
  * 1 tsp Chilli Flakes
  * 2 tsp Tomato Sauce
  * 2 tsp Chilli Sauce
  * 1 tsp Vinegar
  * 2 tbsp Honey
  * 1 tsp Salt
  * 4 tsp Sesame Seeds , roasted
  * 2 Bulbs Spring Onions

## How to Make Honey Chilli Potato

HideShow Media

### For Frying Potatoes:

1.

Take two potatoes and cut them into slices in a bowl.

2.

In the bowl add corn flour, salt and red chilli powder.

3.

Toss them well so that the mixture coats the sliced potatoes properly.

4.

Now heat oil in a broad pan and deep fry the potatoes on medium flame till
golden brown and crisp.

5.

Do not fry on high flame otherwise they will burn from outside and remain
uncooked from inside.

6.

In another pan dry roast the sesame seeds on low heat till light brown and
keep aside.

### For Preparing Honey Chilli Sauce:

1.

Heat oil and add garlic, ginger, whole red chillies, chilli flakes and tomato
sauce. Stir well.

2.

Now add chilli sauce, vinegar, honey and salt. Mix all the ingredients well to
make a sauce.

3.

Add the fried potatoes and coat them well with the sauce.

4.

Toss well and sprinkle the roasted sesame seeds and spring onions.

5.

Combine the ingredients well and serve the crunchy honey chilli potatoes
immediately.

Key Ingredients: Potatoes, Corn flour, Salt, Chilli Powder, Garlic, Ginger,
Whole Red Chillies , Chilli Flakes, Tomato Sauce, Chilli Sauce, Vinegar,
Honey, Salt, Sesame Seeds , Spring Onions
