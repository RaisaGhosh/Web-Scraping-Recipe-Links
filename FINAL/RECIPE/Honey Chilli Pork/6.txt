#### 9 Ingredients

  * 1 tbsp olive oil 

  * 500g pkt Coles Pork Belly Bites 

  * 1 tbsp soy sauce 

  * 1 tbsp sweet chilli sauce 

  * 1 tbsp honey 

  * 1 garlic clove 

  * 2 tsp finely grated ginger 

  * 1 spring onion, thinly sliced 

  * 1 red chilli, thinly sliced (optional)

#### 3 Method Steps

  * Step 1

Heat the oil  (1 tbsp olive oil) in a large frying pan over high heat. Cook
pork  (500g pkt Coles Pork Belly Bites), turning, for 5 mins or until golden
and heated through.

  * Step 2

Add the soy sauce  (1 tbsp soy sauce), sweet chilli sauce  (1 tbsp sweet
chilli sauce), honey  (1 tbsp honey), garlic  (1 garlic clove) and ginger  (2
tsp finely grated ginger) to the pan. Cook, tossing, for 3-5 mins or until the
pork is coated in sauce and the sauce is sticky.

  * Step 3

Arrange the pork on a serving plate. Drizzle with a little sauce and sprinkle
with spring onion  (1 spring onion, thinly sliced) and chilli  (1 red chilli,
thinly sliced (optional)), if using.
