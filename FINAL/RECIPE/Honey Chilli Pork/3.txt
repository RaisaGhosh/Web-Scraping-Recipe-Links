# Sizzling honey and chilli pork

Feb 27, 2014 1:00pm

  * 5 mins preparation
  * 15 mins cooking
  * 3 hrs marinating
  * Serves 6

Print

### Ingredients

Marinade

  * 1/2 cup (90g) honey
  * 1/4 cup (95g) hoisin sauce
  * 1 tablespoon lee kum kee chilli garlic sauce
  * 1 teaspoon grated ginger

Sizzling pork

  * 1 kilogram pork fillets, trimmed
  * 2 teaspoon peanut oil

### Method

Sizzling honey and chilli pork

  * 1

Combine honey, hoisin sauce, chilli garlic sauce and ginger in a bowl. Add
pork fillets and turn to coat well. Chill for 3 hours or overnight.

  * 2

Preheat chargrill plate on medium. Brush with peanut oil. Remove pork from
marinade. Chargrill for 15-20 minutes, turning and basting, until caramelised
and cooked through.

  * 3

Cover with foil and rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
