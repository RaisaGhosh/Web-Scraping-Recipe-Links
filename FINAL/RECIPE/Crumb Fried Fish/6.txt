##  Directions

Instructions Checklist

  * Step 1

Preheat an air fryer to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).

Advertisement

  * Step 2

Place bread crumbs and oil into a shallow bowl; stir until mixture becomes
loose and crumbly.

  * Step 3

Dip fish fillets into egg; shake off any excess. Dip fillets into bread crumb
mixture; coat evenly and fully.

  * Step 4

Lay coated fillets gently in the air fryer basket; cook in the preheated air
fryer until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 12 minutes. Garnish with
lemon slices; serve.
