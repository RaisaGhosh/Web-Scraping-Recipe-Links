# Fried Fish with Tartar Sauce Recipe

  * Niru Gupta

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Fried Fish with Tartar Sauce

Fish fillets marinated with salt and lemon juice. Coated with flour,
breadcrumbs, eggs and deep fried till golden. Served with a creamy tartar
sauce.

  * Total Cook Time1 hr 10 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time1 hr 

  * Recipe Servings8

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Fried Fish with Tartar Sauce**

  * 8 fish fillets
  * 1 tsp salt
  * 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  * 1 egg-slightly beaten
  * 1/4 cup flour
  * 1/4 tsp salt
  * 1/4 tsp black pepper powdered
  * Bread crumbs
  * Oil for deep frying
  * For the Tartar Sauce:
  * 1 cup mayonnaise
  * 1 onion-chopped fine
  * 1 tomato, de-seeded and chopped very fine
  * 2 Tbsp chopped celery
  * 1/4 tsp salt

## How to Make Fried Fish with Tartar Sauce

HideShow Media

1.

Marinate the fish in the salt and lemon juice for half an hour.

2.

Wash the fish and wipe dry and dip it in the seasoned flour, dusting off any
excess flour.

3.

Now dip the fish into the beaten egg and then into the powdered breadcrumbs,
taking care not to wet the rest of the crumbs.

4.

Repeat the egg and crumb treatment once more and keep the fish aside till
ready to fry.

5.

Mix the sauce ingredients together, for the Tartar Sauce.

6.

Just before serving, heat the oil and deep-fry the fish to a golden brown.
