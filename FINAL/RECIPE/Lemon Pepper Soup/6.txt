# Vegetable Pepper And Lemon: A Diet Soup Recipe

Vegetarian Soup

oi-Sowmya

By **Sowmya Shekar**

on June 1, 2016

If you are dieting and meanwhile finding it really hard to stay away from
food, then we have good news for you.! For those who are on diet, the best
option for them is to take a sip of the vegetable lemon and pepper soup. This
soup not only tastes great but it is a very healthy soup.

For the vegetable lemon soup, we add the different flavours of spices along
with healthy vegetables.

The recipe is also best suited for kids too. You can serve this soup as a
starter or as a heathy drink that you can have anytime of the day.

So, let's take a look at how to prepare this healthy vegetable lemon and
pepper soup drink.

**Serves -4**

Cooking time- 15 minutes

Preperation time - 15 minutes

**Ingredients**

Carrot - 1 cup (Chopped)

Onions -1 cup (Chopped)

Capsicum -1 cup (Chopped)

Spring Onions - 1 cup (Chopped)

Cabbage -1 cup (Chopped)

Garlic- 1/4 Teaspoon

Ginger - 1/4 Teaspoon (Chopped)

Corn Flour - 3 Teaspoon

Pepper - 1/2 Teaspoon

Lemon Juice- 2 Teaspoon

Vegetable Stalk- 2 Cups

Oil

Salt

**Procedure**

  1. Take a pan and add oil. Once heated, add ginger and garlic and saute them.
  2. Then add onions, carrots, capsicum, cabbage and spring onions.
  3. Saute them well. Now add the vegetable stalk.
  4. Then add pepper and salt.
  5. Keep stirring.
  6. Now mix the corn flour with some water to make it a paste.
  7. Then add this paste to the vegetable soup.
  8. Now add lemon juice and continue to stir it for five minutes.
  9. Then let it boil for five to ten minutes.
  10. Sprinkle coriander leaves on the soup and serve the hot and healthy soup.

Try the vegetable pepper and lemon soup recipe and let us know your feedback.
