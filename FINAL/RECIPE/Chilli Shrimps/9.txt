# Red Chili Shrimp Stir-Fry

featured in Shrimp Stir-Fry 4 Ways

Joey Firoben

Tasty Team

  * Share via pinterest
  * Share via facebook
  * Share via email
  * Share via sms
  * Print

## Ingredients

for 4 servings

  * 1 lb large shrimp (455 g), peeled and deveined
  * 1 red bell pepper, diced
  * ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  * 1 tablespoon low sodium soy sauce
  * 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  * ½ teaspoon salt, divided
  * ¼ cup green onion (25 g), sliced, for topping

## Nutrition Info

## Preparation

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of sesame oil in a large, nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Pour in the shrimp and season with ¼ teaspoon of the salt.
  3. Fry the shrimp for 1 minute on each side.

  4. Remove the shrimp from the pan and pour in the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil.
  5. Toss in the red bell pepper, remaining ¼ teaspoon of salt, and red pepper flakes and sauté for a few minutes until they begin to soften.
  6. Add in the soy sauce and sauté about 1 more minute.

  7. Toss the shrimp back into the pan and mix until everything is well incorporated and the shrimp is fully cooked, about 1 minute.
  8. Divide the mixture evenly between 4 bowls and top with green onion.
  9. Enjoy!
