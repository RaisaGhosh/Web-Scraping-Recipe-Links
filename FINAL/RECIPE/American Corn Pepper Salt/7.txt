# Masala Sweet Corn Recipe

 _Masala sweet corn chaat is very simple and quick to make, in this American
chaat sweet corn is sauteed in the butter adding various spices like black
pepper, salt, garam masala and chili flakes. At last to give it little tangy
taste lemon juice is added. Masala sweet corn is an ideal snack idea for
evening hunger pangs or even picnic or potlucks. Learn how to make masala
sweet corn._

 **Total Time: 15 mins**

 **Serves: 3 - 4 **

  

**Ingredients:**

  * 2 cups boiled sweet
  * 1 tsp butter
  * 1 tbsp lemon juice
  * 1/2tsp chili flakes
  * 1/2tsp black pepper
  * Pinch of garam masala
  * Salt to taste

 **How to make American Masala Sweet Corn :**

  1. Wash the corn properly.
  2. Melt butter in a wok; add sweet corn along with chili flakes, black pepper, garam masala and salt.
  3. At last sprinkle lemon juice over it and mix all the ingredients well. Saute if for a minute.
  4. Take them out in small cups and serve hot.
