# Homemade Ginger Ale Recipe

  * Shashank Sharma 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Homemade Ginger Ale

 **About Homemade Ginger Ale Recipe** : Do you like ginger ale? Here is a
super-quick recipe to make it at home. The freshness of mint juice, lemon
juice and ginger are sure to beat the summer blues.

  * Total Cook Time 15 mins 

  * Prep Time 05 mins 

  * Cook Time 10 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Homemade Ginger Ale**

  * 30 Ml mint juice (method follows)
  * 15 ml ginger juice
  * 20 ml lemon juice
  * 15 ml sugar syrup
  * soda
  * Sprig of mint

## How to Make Homemade Ginger Ale

HideShow Media

1.

To make the mint juice, take mint leaves and ice and blend the two. Strain out
the juice.

2.

To make ginger juice, use ginger and a little bit of water and blend it

3.

Mix mint juice, ginger juice, lemon juice, and the sugar syrup in a tall
glass.

4.

Top it up with soda. Garnish with a spring of mint.

5.

Serve chilled.

Key Ingredients: mint juice (method follows), ginger juice, lemon juice, sugar
syrup, soda, Sprig of mint
