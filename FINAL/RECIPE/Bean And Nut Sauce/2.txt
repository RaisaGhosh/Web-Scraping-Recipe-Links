# Asian Black Bean Sauce Recipe

  * Niru Gupta

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Asian Black Bean Sauce

 **About Asian Black Bean Sauce Recipe** : Asian black bean sauce is full of
flavour and uses black beans, peanuts and rice wine. With just a few simple
ingredients and 10 minutes, you can prepare this delicious Asian sauce and use
it as a topping for many oriental dishes.

  * Total Cook Time 25 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 15 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Asian Black Bean Sauce**

  * 2 Tbsp peanut Oil
  * 2 tbsp black beans (fermented, rinsed and drained)
  * 1 tbsp garlic (minced)
  * 1/2 tbsp chicken broth
  * 1 tbsp soy Sauce
  * 2 tbsp rice Wine
  * 1 tsp sugar
  * 1 1/2 tsp cornstarch

## How to Make Asian Black Bean Sauce

HideShow Media

1.

Heat a wok or heavy skillet over medium-high heat.

2.

Add the oil, the black beans and garlic. Stir-fry about 12 seconds.

3.

Add the rest of the ingredients and stir-fry about 1 minute or until sauce
thickens.

4.

Makes about 3/4 cup sauce.

5.

Store and serve whenever required.

Key Ingredients: peanut Oil, black beans (fermented, rinsed and drained),
garlic (minced), chicken broth, soy Sauce, rice Wine, sugar, cornstarch
