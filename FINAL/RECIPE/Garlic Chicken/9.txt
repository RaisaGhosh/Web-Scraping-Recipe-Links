# EASY  
GARLIC BUTTER CHICKEN  
RECIPE

# Ingredients

200 gm Boneless Chicken  

1 Egg  

Â¼ cup Onion  

2 tbsp Butter  

# Ingredients

2 tsp Garlic  

1 tbsp Spring Onion

1 tbsp Maida  

1 tsp Corn Flour  

# Ingredients

1 tsp Red Chilli Flakes  

 1 tsp Black Pepper Powder

Salt to taste  

1 tsp Oregano  

In a bowl, add boneless chicken, salt, black pepper, egg, and mix well. Now
add the maida. Mix and keep it aside for 15 minutes. Fry until golden brown.  

##  Step 1

In another pan add butter, garlic, onion, red chili flakes, oregano, salt, and
black pepper and mix well.  

##  Step 2

Add corn flour slurry and cook for 2 minutes. Then add water and let it
simmer.  

##  Step 3

Add the fried chicken, spring onion. Cook for 2 minutes.  

##  Step 4

Serve hot.
