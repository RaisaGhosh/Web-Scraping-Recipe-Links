# How to make Street-Style Chicken Lollipop

## ​Chicken Lollipop

Street-Style Chicken Lollipop is a must-try snack for those who love crunchy
snacks with a lot of spices. Try this simplest Chicken Lollipop recipe at home
and enjoy!

istock

## ​Ingredients required

10 pieces chicken lollipop, oil for deep frying, 2 tbsp red chilli paste, 1
tbsp ginger paste, 1 tbsp garlic paste, 1 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp pepper
powder, salt to taste, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp vinegar, 3 tbsp tomato
ketchup, 3 tbsp corn flour, 3 tbsp all-purpose flour, and a pinch of
ajinomoto.

istock

## ​Step 1

Wash the chicken lollipop well and set aside.

istock

## Step 2

In a mixing bowl, add red chili paste, ginger paste, garlic paste, chilli
powder, pepper powder, salt, soy sauce, vinegar, and tomato ketchup. Mix them
well.

pexels

## ​Step 3

Now, add corn flour, maida (all-purpose flour) and ajinomoto to the mixture
and gently bind all the ingredients.

istock

## ​Step 4

Now, add the washed chicken lollipop in the mixture and make sure they are
coated well.

istock

## ​Step 5

Heat enough oil in a frying pan and drop marinated chicken lollipop in hot oil
and cook on low heat for 10 mins till the chicken is cooked.

istock

## ​Step 6

Now, increase the heat and fry the pieces for 5 minutes or till they turn
crispy and golden brown.

istock

## ​Step 7

Place them on a kitchen towel or paper. Serve hot with the dip of your choice.
