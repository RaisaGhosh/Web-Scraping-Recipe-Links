# Hot Garlic chicken

A familiar Chinese entrée loved and consumed almost everywhere in the country.
Here is a easy way to whiff up a bowl of this awesomeness anytime you want.

  * 

###  Recipe Alergies

  * _N-Veg_ Non- Vegetarian

### Chef Solutions Ingredients used

### Other Ingredients Used

  * Chicken thigh deboned: 300 gm 
  * Garlic chopped : 30 gms 
  * Chopped red chili : 20 gm 
  * Chopped green chili : 10gm 
  * Sesame oil : 5ml 
  * Corn flour : 3+2 tbspn 
  * Eggs : 4 
  * Chili oil : 3ml 

### Steps Involved in Cooking:

  * _1 ._ Wash and pat dry the chicken pieces. 
  * _2 ._ In a mixing bowl add the chicken, sesame oil, chili oil, 10 gms of garlic and mix well 
  * _3 ._ Beat 4 eggs and add the chicken pieces to it and coat with corn flour 
  * _4 ._ Deep fry and set aside on kitchen towels 
  * _5 ._ Make corn flour and water slurry 
  * _6 ._ In a wok or tossing pan add oil and sauté red and green chili. 
  * _7 ._ Add the remaining chopped garlic and sauté till golden 
  * _8 ._ Add the hot garlic sauce and add water till submerged 
  * _9 ._ Add the chicken pieces and reduce till the gravy is thick. Use slurry only if the gravy is not thick enough 
  * _10 ._ Adjust seasoning and serve hot

which can be made by using CS ingredients:

### Pointers

  * Use slurry only if the gravy isn’t thick enough.
  * In case chicken thighs are not available
  * chicken breast nuggets can be used.
