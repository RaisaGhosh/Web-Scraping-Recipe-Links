# Sweet & sour prawns with green beans

#### 14 Ingredients

  * 2 tbsp Coles Brand Pure Olive Oil 

  * 1/2 brown onion, finely chopped 

  * 1/2 red chilli, thinly sliced 

  * 2 tbsp ginger, finely chopped 

  * 1 cup fresh pineapple, finely chopped 

  * 1/4 cup rice wine vinegar 

  * 1/4 cup tomato sauce 

  * 2 tbsp Coles Brand Soy Sauce 

  * 1/4 cup brown sugar 

  * 3/4 cup fresh pineapple juice 

  * 500g raw banana prawns, peeled, deveined, tail left on 

  * 2 cups green beans 

  * 1 tbsp coriander, chopped 

  * Steamed white rice, to serve

#### 4 Method Steps

  * Step 1

To make sauce, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium
heat and cook onion for 3 mins. Add chilli and ginger and cook for a further 2
mins, then add pineapple and cook for a further minute.

  * Step 2

Add vinegar, tomato sauce, soy sauce, sugar and pineapple juice and bring to a
simmer. Simmer for 6-7 mins, or until sauce begins to thicken. Remove from the
heat.

  * Step 3

Place a large frying pan over high heat and add remaining oil. Once oil begins
to smoke, add prawns and cook for 2 mins, stirring occasionally. Add beans and
cook for a further 2 mins, then season lightly with salt and pepper.

  * Step 4

Add sweet and sour sauce and toss well to coat prawns and green beans. Garnish
with chopped coriander and serve with steamed rice.
