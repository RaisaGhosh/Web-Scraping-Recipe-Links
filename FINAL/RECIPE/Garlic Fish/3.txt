# Chilli Garlic Fish Recipe

  * Vijay Anand Bakshi 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

A great appetizer which blends the aroma of Indian spices along with sesame
seeds and the delicate flavour of Basa.

  * Total Cook Time 30 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 20 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Chilli Garlic Fish**

  * 3 Tbsp tahini paste
  * 2 Tbsp garlic, chopped
  * 1 tsp green chili, chopped
  * 1 tsp chili flakes
  * 30 ml sunflower oil
  * 1 tsp celery, chopped
  * 1/2 tsp dagad phool
  * Salt as per taste
  * 1 tsp sesame seeds
  * 2 tsp pomegranate powder
  * 500 gm boneless basa fish

## How to Make Chilli Garlic Fish

HideShow Media

1.

Debone the fish.

2.

Cut rectangular pieces I inch thickness and 3 inch in length, set aside.

3.

In a mixing bowl place tahini, oil, mix well

4.

And add garlic, chili flakes, green chili, celery, salt,

5.

Dagad phool, pomegranate mix well with a balloon whisk.

6.

Marinate the fish and set aside in a refrigerator at least for 2 hours.

7.

Skewer and grill on a moderate hot grill, cook till the fish is cooked from
inside.

8.

Serve hot.
