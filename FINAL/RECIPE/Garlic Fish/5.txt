# About Garlic Butter Fish Recipe:

Garlic butter fish is one of the best fish recipes pan-fried with garlic
butter sauce. This garlic butter fish recipe takes 20 mins. Serve alone or
with pasta for a wholesome dinner.

## Ingredients for Garlic Butter Fish Recipe

  * 4 ounce Firm white fish fillet
  * As required Salt
  * As required Ground black pepper
  * 1 dash Cayenne pepper
  * 0.67 tablespoon Corn starch
  * 0.67 tablespoon Cooking Oil
  * As required Lemon wedges
  * 0.17 stick Salted butter, melted
  * 1  Cloves Garlic, Minced
  * 0.17 tablespoon Lemon juice
