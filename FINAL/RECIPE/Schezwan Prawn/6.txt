## Ingredients for Prawns In Schezwan Sauce Recipe

  * 10  Prawns cleaned and deveined
  * As required TO MARINATE PRAWNS
  * 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  * As required Salt
  * 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  * 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
  * 1/2 teaspoon Corn flour
  * 1/4 teaspoon Lemon juice
  * As required oil to fry
  * As required FOR THE SAUCE
  * 3/4 tablespoon Scheshwan sauce
  * 1  onion , medium,chopped
  * 1/2  Capsicum,chopped
  * 1/2  ginger small piece,chopped
  * 2.50  garlic , cloves ,chopped
  * 3/4 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  * 1 pinch pepper
  * 1  Green chillies ,chopped
  * 1/2 teaspoon Light soy sauce
  * 1 pinch Sugar
  * 1/2 teaspoon Corn flour
  * 1/2 cup water + 2tbsp
  * 1 tablespoon Oil
  * As required For decoration
  * 1.50 tablespoon Spring onion,chopped
