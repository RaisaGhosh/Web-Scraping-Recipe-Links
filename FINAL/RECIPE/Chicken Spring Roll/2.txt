# Chicken Spring Roll Recipe

  * Recipe in Hindi
  * Review 

  * Overview

  * Method

How to make Chicken Spring Roll

Treat your guests with these must-have, with a juicy surprise in them. Try
making the classic chicken spring roll with this recipe here

  * Total Cook Time 30 mins 

  * Prep Time 15 mins 

  * Cook Time 15 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Chicken Spring Roll**

  * 6-7 Boneless Chicken Pieces
  * 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste 
  * 1 small Chopped onion
  * 1 small chopped capsicum 
  * 1/2 cup Cabbage
  * 1 tsp Red chilli powder 
  * 1/2 tsp Pepper 
  * Salt as per taste 
  * 1/2 cup All-purpose flour 
  * 1/2 cup Cornflour
  * Water 

## How to Make Chicken Spring Roll

HideShow Media

1.

Add some oil, shredded chicken pieces in a pan, and cook it. Then take out the
chicken.

2.

Add chopped ginger-garlic, onion, capsicum, and cabbage in the same pan. Mix
it well.

3.

Throw in the cooked chicken pieces and add red chilli powder, salt, pepper,
and mix.

4.

Once it is cooked, keep it aside.

5.

For preparing the sheet, add all-purpose flour, cornflour, salt, and water in
a bowl.

6.

Make a runny batter. Then, heat a pan, and pour a ladle full of it.

7.

Spread evenly and cook slightly. Repeat to make the spring roll sheets.

8.

Once they are ready, add in the filling, roll it and fry till a crisp!

9.

Take out fresh spring rolls and enjoy!

Key Ingredients: Boneless Chicken Pieces, Ginger garlic paste , Chopped onion,
chopped capsicum , Cabbage, Red chilli powder , Pepper , Salt as per taste ,
All-purpose flour , Cornflour, Water
