##  Lemon Coriander Soup recipe - How to make Lemon Coriander Soup

## Tags

Chinese SoupsChinese Party

Preparation Time: 10 mins.  &nbspCooking; Time: 15 mins.  &nbspTotal; Time: 25
mins     4Serves 4.  
Show me for servings

Ingredients  

  
  
For the stock  
2 to 3 whole lemongrass (hare chai ki patti), washed  
1 to 2 green chillies , slit  
  
Other ingredients  
1/4 cup carrots, sliced  
1/4 cup mushrooms (khumbh), sliced  
1/4 cup cabbage cubes  
1/4 cup red cabbage cubes  
1/4 cup sliced spring onion whites  
2 tbsp chopped spring onion greens  
1 tsp oil  
salt and black pepper (kalimirch) powder to taste  
  
For the garnish  
1 tbsp chopped coriander (dhania)  
1 tsp lemon juice  

Method

Main Procedure

  
For the stock  

For the stock

  1. Combine the lemon grass and chilles in a pan with 6 cups of water and bring to a boil. 
  2. Simmer till the water reduces to approximately 4 cups. Strain and keep the stock aside, discarding the lemon grass and chillies. 

  
How to proceed  

How to proceed

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and add the carrots, mushrooms, cabbage, red cabbage and spring onion whites and saut√© for 1 to 2 minutes. 
  2. Add the stock, salt and pepper and bring to a boil 
  3. Simmer till the vegetables are tender. 
  4. Add the spring onion greens and serve hot garnished with coriander and lemon juice.
