# Chicken Momos Recipe

  * Kamna Saksena 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Chicken Momos

Momos are a type of dumplings which can either be steamed or fried. This
delicious appetizer is native to the Himalayas of Nepal and popularly eaten as
a street snack with fiery red chilli sauce.

  * Total Cook Time 30 mins 

  * Cook Time 30 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Chicken Momos**

  * 500 gms chicken, minced
  * 200 gms cabbage
  * 100 gms carrot
  * 3-4 cloves of fresh garlic
  * 2 tsp soya sauce
  * Salt to taste
  * Wontons ready made coverings
  * Cooking oil spray or 1 tsp cooking oil
  * One steamer
  * Chilli oil / chilli sauce for serving

## How to Make Chicken Momos

HideShow Media

1.

Grind all the above ingredients in a blender until it attains a granular
consistency paste.

2.

On a chopping board, sprinkle some water so that the board is a bit damp, now
lay down the wonton coverings one by one and put small teaspoon of the above
paste on all individually.

3.

Close each covering in a round or semi circle shape as you like.

4.

Spray a little bit of cooking oil on the steamer's surface and lay down the
dumplings.

5.

Allow it to steam for 7-10 minutes. Serve it with chilli oil or chilli sauce
as you like.
