# Vegetable Chopsuey Recipe

  * Niru Gupta

  * Recipe in Hindi
  * Review

How To Make Vegetable Chopsuey

 **About Vegetable Chopsuey Recipe** : A popular fusion chinese dish, chopsuey
is that delicious option that is loved by kids and adults alike. An all time
favorite Chinese dish that never fails to delight, you can serve this chopsuey
for brunch, lunch or even as a light snack for kids at a birthday party. Just
a 40 minute recipe and you can cook this easy and quick vegetable chopsuey at
home.

 **Ingredients in Vegetable Chopsuey Recipe** : Saucy vegetables like carrots,
cabbage and capsicums are sauteed with soya sauce and topped with crispy fried
noodles.

  * Total Cook Time 50 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 40 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Vegetable Chopsuey**

  * 1/2 Cup carrots, shredded
  * 1 cup cabbage , shredded
  * 1/2 cup capsicums , shredded
  * 1/2 cup onions , sliced
  * 2 tsp garlic, finely chopped
  * 2 tbsp oil
  * 200 gms noodles (drained well), boiled
  * 2 tbsp vinegar
  * 1/2 tsp soya sauce
  * 1 tsp sugar
  * to taste salt
  * 2 tbsp cornflour
  * Oil (to deep fry)

## How to Make Vegetable Chopsuey

HideShow Media

1.

Mix together the vinegar, soya sauce, sugar, salt and cornflour and add enough
water to make up to a cup. Keep aside.

2.

Heat the 2 Tbsp oil and add the onions and garlic.

3.

Saute till a little soft. Add the carrots, cabbage and capsicum and stir-fry
over high heat for a minute.

4.

Add the water mixture and cook, stirring continuously. Keep aside.

5.

Deep fry the noodles over high heat till brown. Drain.

6.

Transfer noodles to a serving dish, pour the vegetables over and serve.

Key Ingredients: carrots, cabbage , capsicums , onions , garlic, oil, noodles
(drained well), vinegar, soya sauce, sugar, salt, cornflour, Oil (to deep fry)
