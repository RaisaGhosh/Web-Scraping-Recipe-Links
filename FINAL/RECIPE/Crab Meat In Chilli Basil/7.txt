## Thai Recipes : Thai Spicy Stir fry Crab Meat

#### * Thai Recipe Ingredients *



* 300 grams cooked crab meat 

* 12-15 garlic cloves, coarsely chopped 

* 2 tablespoon chilies, coarsely chopped 

* 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper 

* 1 tablespoon oyster sauce 





* 1 teaspoon sugar 

* 1 tablespoon soy sauce 

* 1 1/2 tablespoons fish sauce 

* 3 tablespoons oil

* fried basil leaves for garnishing 



### Thai Food Preparations

1\. Heat oil in a wok over medium heat. Add garlics and chilies. Stir fry
until fragrant. Then add crab meat, and stir softly.





2\. Season with ground pepper, oyster sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce and sugar.
Turn up to high heat and stir quickly until all ingredients mixed well. Remove
from heat.



3\. Transfer to a serving dish. Garnish on top with fried basil leaves. Serve
immdeiately with hot steamed rice.





(For 2 Serving)
