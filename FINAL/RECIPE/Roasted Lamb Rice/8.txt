# About Lamb & Rice Recipe:

The sauce in this recipe is a heavenly mixture of tomatoes, spices, herbs and
mustard. It provides amazing flavor to the lamb as they spend up to 12 hours
together in the slow cooker.

  * 0.17 teaspoon Ground coriander
  * 0.08 teaspoon Dry Mustard
  * 1/25 teaspoon Salt
  * 1/50 teaspoon Cayenne pepper
  * 0.33 cup Hot cooked brown rice
