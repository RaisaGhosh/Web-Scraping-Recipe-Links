# Rice in Lamb Stock Recipe

  * NDTV Food 

  * Recipe in Hindi
  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time

  * Method

How to make Rice in Lamb Stock

 **About Rice in Lamb Stock Recipe** : Aromatic rice cooked with cashew nuts,
cream, cardamom powder and a flavorsome lamb stock.

  * Total Cook Time1 hr 25 mins 

  * Prep Time 15 mins 

  * Cook Time1 hr 10 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Rice in Lamb Stock**

  * 1 Cup rice
  * 1 tbsp desi ghee
  * 3-4 black cardamoms
  * 3 bay leaves
  * 4-5 cinnamon sticks
  * 1 tsp fennel seeds
  * 1 onion, sliced
  * 1/2 kg lamb bones
  * 1 tsp royal cumin seeds
  * 1/2 cup cashew nuts
  * 1 cup cream
  * 1 tsp green cardamom powder
  * Salt
  * Water

## How to Make Rice in Lamb Stock

HideShow Media

1.

Soak the rice in water for twenty minutes.

2.

Heat desi ghee in a pan and add black cardamoms, bay leaves, cinnamon and
fennel seeds.

3.

Once the spices begin to splutter, add sliced onion and saute. When the onions
turn brown, add lamb bones, salt and water. Stir well and allow it to simmer
for sometime.

4.

Once the stock is ready, take out the bones and strain the liquid in a utensil
in which the rice is to be cooked.

5.

Now heat desi ghee in a pan, add royal cumin seeds, cashew nuts, cream, green
cardamom powder and the lamb stock.

6.

As it boils, add the rice and cook.

7.

Serve rice hot.

Key Ingredients: rice, desi ghee, black cardamoms, bay leaves, cinnamon
sticks, fennel seeds, onion, lamb bones, royal cumin seeds, cashew nuts,
cream, green cardamom powder, Salt, Water
