#  Prepare Crispy Lamb Beijing Style in 5 Simple Steps

October 2, 2015 __1700 __0 __0

Spread the love

  *  __
  *  __
  *  __
  *  __
  *  __
  *  __

Crispy lamb Beijing style is a must try dish for all lamb-lovers. The
crispiness of the meat and the sourness of garlic make it a palatable delight.
It might not be one of the best looking lamp dishes but its taste is certainly
unbeatable.

Source: gowstakeout.com

**Ingredients:**

  * 250 gm ( 10 oz) lamb, finely shredded

  * 20 gm ( 1 oz) carrot, diced

  * 20 gm (1 oz) capsicum, diced

  * 25 gm (1.2 oz) onion, diced 

  * 25 gm (1.2 oz) spring onion, diced

  * 1 tablespoon oil ½ teaspoon ginger, chopped 

  * ½ teaspoon garlic , chopped

  * 1 ½ teaspoons chilli paste 

  * 50 ml (1.5 fl oz) tomato ketchup

  * 1 teaspoon sesame oil

  * ½ teaspoon salt 

  * ½ teaspoon sugar 

For the Marinade

  * ½ teaspoon salt 

  * 1 tablespoon corn-flour

  * 1 teaspoon oil

For Frying

  * 2 tablespoon corn-flour

  * 250 ml (8 fl oz) oil

**Method:**

1\. Marinade the lamb for at least 2 hours.

2\. Coat it with additional corn-flour and deep-fry till golden brown and
cooked.

3\. In a separate pan, heat oil, sauté ginger, garlic, chilli paste, add the
meat, toss, add diced vegetables and toss again.

4\. Add tomato ketchup and seasoning.

Source: foodieinternational.com

5\. Finish with sesame oil and serve hot.
