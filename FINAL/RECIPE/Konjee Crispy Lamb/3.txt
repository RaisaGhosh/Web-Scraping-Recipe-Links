# Stir Frid Crispy Lamb

Crispy Lamb

  * **35 Min** Prep Time 
  * **35 Min** Cooking time 
  * ** 1 Servings **

  * __

__

#### Share this on

  * __Facebook
  * __What's app

##  Ingredients

  * **Lamb Leg Cut Into Fine Strips** • 100 gr
  * **Corn Flour** • 150 gr
  * **Frying Oil** • 1 lt
  * **Egg** • 2 each
  * **Chili** • 10gr
  * **Onion Red Julienne** • 30 gr
  * **Spring Onion Julienne** • 5 gr
  * **Celery Green** • 30 gr
  * **Passion Fruit Juice** • 20 ml
  * **Kikkoman Soy Sauce** • 20 ml
  * **Chinese Black Vinegar** • 20 ml
  * **Dark Soy Sauce** • 10 ml
  * **Sugar** • 30 gr
  * **Coriander** • 5 gr
  * **Sesame Seeds** • 2 gr

##  Method

  * # Step 1

Cut The Lamb Into Fine Strips, Break The Egg And Whisk , Mix The Lamb Strips
With Egg And Corn Starch Till Is Well Adhered To The Meat Bring The Oil Up To
Heat And Fry Well The Lamb Till Cooked , Set Aside To Cool

  * # Step 2

Heat Up The Sunflower Oil Well And Deep Fry Till Crisp The Lamb

  * # Step 3

In A Wok Add Oil, Onion And Celery And Stir Well Few Seconds , Add The Lamb
Strips Pre Fried And Stir Well Add Passion Fruit Juice And The Sauces One By
One Bringing To Boil The Saue Should Ticken , Then At The End Add The Spring
Onion

  * # Step 4

Pour In A Serving Plate, Sprinkle With Sesame Seeds And Decorate With Some
Coriander Leaves
