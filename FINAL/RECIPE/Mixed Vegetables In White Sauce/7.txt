# Mixed vegetables in cheese sauce

#### 6 Ingredients

  * 600g frozen vegetable mix (corn, beans and pumpkin) 

  * 600g frozen cauliflower and broccoli mix 

  * 60g butter 

  * 1/4 cup plain flour 

  * 2 cups milk 

  * 1 cup grated Devondale Tasty Cheese Block (500g) 

  * Select all ingredients 

Add to shopping list

#### 3 Method Steps

  * Step 1

Cook vegetables according to packet directions. Drain.

  * Step 2

Preheat grill on medium-high. Meanwhile, melt butter in a saucepan over medium
heat. Stir in flour. Cook, stirring, for 2 minutes or until bubbling. Remove
from heat. Gradually stir in milk. Return to heat. Cook, stirring, for 5
minutes or until mixture boils and thickens slightly. Remove from heat. Stir
in cheese. Season with salt and pepper.

  * Step 3

Combine vegetables in a lightly greased, 9cm-deep, 8 cup-capacity flame-proof
dish. Pour over cheese sauce. Grill for 5 minutes or until browned. Serve.

### Recipe Notes

**Variation:** You could use the sauce to make cauliflower cheese. Cut 1 small
cauliflower into florets. Cook in a saucepan of boiling water for 10 to 12
minutes or until tender. Continue from step 2. Serve with corned beef or thick
sausages.
