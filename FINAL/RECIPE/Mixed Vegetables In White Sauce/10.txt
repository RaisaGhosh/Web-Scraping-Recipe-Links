# About Mixed Vegetables In Creamy Sauce Recipe:

Mixed vegetables in creamy sauce is a dish that is sure to pamper your palate
with its luscious texture and appealing flavour. Colourful and juicy veggies
are sauted with flavourful ingredients like green chillies and onions, and
cooked with white sauce. The white sauce should neither be too thick or too
thin, in order to get the right consistency. A dash of pepper is all that is
needed to boost the taste of this delectable dish, which is fabulously creamy
even without any cream or cheese being added to it Enjoy it hot, with butter
naan or garlic bread.

## Ingredients for Mixed Vegetables In Creamy Sauce Recipe

  * 1/4 cup Diagonally cut and boiled mixed vegetables
  * 0.67 teaspoon Butter
  * 0.17 cup Finely chopped onions
  * 0.33 teaspoon Finely chopped green chillies
  * 0.17 teaspoon Garlic paste
  * 0.33 cup White sauce
  * As required Salt
  * As required Butter naan
