### Ingredients

  * 10 g - fresh galangal 
  * 1 - egg, for marination 
  * 1 tbsp - corn flour, for marination 
  * 2 - fresh lemon grass, crushed 
  * 5 - kafir lime leaves 
  * 10 - fresh red chillies 
  * 20 - sweet basil leaves 
  * 4 - chopped onions 
  * 1 cup - stock 
  * 180 g - sliced fish 
  * 3 tbsp - fish sauce 
  * 2 tbsp - lemon juice 
  * 2 tbsp - oyster sauce

# How to Make Chilli Basil Fish in Clay Pot

  * Pound the chillies, lemon grass, kafir lime leaves and basil leaves together and keep aside. 
  * Marinate the fish with egg and corn flour and deep-fry in hot oil. 
  * Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a pan and stir-fry the pounded ingredients with sliced ginger and onions. 
  * Add the stock, oyster sauce, fish sauce and bring to boil in a clay pot. 
  * Add fish and finish with lime juice. 
  * Serve hot. |
