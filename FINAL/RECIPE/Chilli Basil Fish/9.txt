# Seafood with Chilli and Basil

_167_ views

Ingredients:  
Mussels – 16, in shells  
Squids – 250 gms, cleaned, cubed  
Oil – 1 tbsp  
Garlic Cloves – 2, chopped  
Spring Onions – 6, sliced  
Basil Leaves – 20, fresh  
Fish Sauce – 2 tbsp  
Soy Sauce – 1 tbsp  
Red/ Green Chilli – 1, deseeded, chopped  
Brown Sugar – 1 tbsp  
Prawns – 200 gms, peeled, uncooked

Method:  
1\. Scrub the mussels, remove any dirt or beard and rinse well.  
2\. Heat the oil in a large saucepan.  
3\. Add the garlic and spring onions.  
4\. Fry for 1 minute.  
5\. Add the remaining ingredients except the squids and prawns.  
6\. Cook covered for 5 to 6 minutes.  
7\. Discard the mussels that do not open.  
8\. Add the squids and prawns.  
9\. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes or till they are cooked.  
10\. Serve hot.
