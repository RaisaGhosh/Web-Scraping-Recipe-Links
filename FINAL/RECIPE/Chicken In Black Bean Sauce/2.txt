How to make Diced Chicken in Black Bean Sauce

 **About Diced Chicken in Black Bean Sauce **: Juicy chicken pieces cooked
with peppers, chillies and black bean sauce.

  * Total Cook Time 40 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 30 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Medium

###  **Ingredients of Diced Chicken in Black Bean Sauce**

  * 250 gms chicken
  * 50 gms red & yellow peppers
  * 10 gms sugar
  * 5 gms salt
  * 10 gms black bean sauce
  * 10 gms chili paste
  * 5 ml soya sauce
  * 10 gms garlic
  * 5 gms red chili , chopped
  * 1 tbsp oil
  * 10 gms corn flour

## How to Make Diced Chicken in Black Bean Sauce

HideShow Media

1.

Slice Chicken. Add oil in wok and mix garlic, chili and add the meat and
saute.

2.

Add black bean and toss away. Add 1 cup of water. Add soya sauce and cook for
a while.

3.

Add corn flour (mixed in a little water). Let the curry thicken and take off
the fire.

4.

Serve Hot.

Key Ingredients: chicken, red & yellow peppers, sugar, salt, black bean sauce,
chili paste, soya sauce, garlic, red chili , oil, corn flour
