How to make Singapore Fried Rice

 **Singapore Fried Rice** This is a spicy fried rice recipe which is made with
various sauces. Veggies give a crunchy twist to the rice. Try this quick &
easy recipe for your next meal.

  * Total Cook Time 25 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 15 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Singapore Fried Rice**

  * 1 Cups Rice
  * 1 Garlic, chopped
  * 2 tbsp olive oil
  * 1/8 tsp pepper
  * 1/2 cups spring onions, chopped
  * 1/2 cups bell peppers
  * 1/4 cups carrots, shredded
  * 1 green chillies
  * 1/2 tbsp vinegar
  * 2 tbsp red chilli sauce or peri peri sauce
  * 1 tbsp light soya sauce
  * 1 tbsp chilli garlic sauce
  * Salt as required

## How to Make Singapore Fried Rice

HideShow Media

1.

Cook the rice al dente and cool it completely.

2.

Heat oil in a wok, saute the garlic to get an aroma. Now add the spring
onions, green chillies and carrots and toss for few minutes.

3.

Add the capsicum and season with salt, stir fry till the veggies are cooked.

4.

Now add the rice to the wok and pour in all the sauces. Season it with pepper
and toss it till it combines.

5.

Singapore fried rice is ready!

Key Ingredients: Rice, Garlic, olive oil, pepper, spring onions, bell peppers,
carrots, green chillies, vinegar, red chilli sauce or peri peri sauce, light
soya sauce, chilli garlic sauce, Salt as required
