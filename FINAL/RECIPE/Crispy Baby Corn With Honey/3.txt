# About Crispy Honey Chilli Baby Corn Recipe:

Simple, tasty, flavorful and easy indo-chinese starter recipe. Crispy baby
corn tossed in a sweet & spicy sauce along with cubed bell peppers & onions.

## Ingredients for Crispy Honey Chilli Baby Corn Recipe

  * 2.50-3  Babycorns
  * 1/2 tablespoon corn flour
  * 1/4 tablespoon Rice flour
  * 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder
  * As required Salt
  * 1/4-1/2 tablespoon Water
  * 1/4  capsicum medium, cubed
  * 1/2  onion medium, cubed
  * 1/4 teaspoon Grated ginger
  * 1/4 teaspoon Sliced garlic
  * 1  Chopped Green chillies
  * 1/4 flakes chilli tsp
  * 1/4 teaspoon Vinegar
  * 1/4 tablespoon honey
  * 1/2 tablespoon chilli sauce
  * As required Salt
