# Restaurant Style Crispy Honey Chilli Baby Corn

Suganya Prakash kid's snacks, kids recipe, Starter recipe, Veg recipe
September 25, 2018December 19, 2018 1 Minute

It is a lip smacking Indo-Chinese starter recipe. It is a semi-dry dish and it
is little spicy, sour and sweet, which addicts our tongue to this recipe. In
this recipe, I have not added capsicum here. Add capsicum if you want. Try
this recipe and share your experience with me…

If you like this recipe, share it to your friends by clicking share button..



Preparation Time: 15 mins  
Cooking Time: 35 mins  
Serves: 4 to 5

**Ingredients:**  
**(1 cup = 200 ML)**  
Baby corn – 200 gms or 20 to 25 pieces  
Garlic – 1 ½ tbsp (finely chopped)  
Green chilli – 2 finely (chopped)  
Honey - 2 tbsp  
Red chilli powder – 3 tsp  
Pepper powder – 1 tsp  
All purpose flour – 1 cup  
Corn flour – 1 cup + 1 tbsp  
Soy sauce – 1 ½ tbsp  
Tomato sauce – 1 tbsp  
Sesame seeds – ½ tbsp  
Vinegar – 1 tbsp  
Spring onion – ½ cup+ 1tbsp for garnishing  
Salt as needed  
Water as needed  
Oil for deep-frying

**Cooking method:**

  * Wash and cut the baby corn in lengthwise.
  * Take a wide bowl; drop the baby corns and sprinkle 1 tbsp all-purpose flour, 1 tsp chilli powder, pepper powder and little salt.
  * Give a gentle mix and keep it aside.
  * Again, take a wide bowl; add remaining all purpose flour, 1-cup corn flour, 1 tsp chilli powder and required salt for making slurry. Pour 1 to 1 ½ cup of water to get thick slurry.
  * Dip each baby corn in the slurry and deep-fry it in a medium flame until it gets crispy texture. Then transfer them to a plate.
  * Place a frying pan, pour 3 tbsp oil. Add chopped garlic, green chillies and spring onions. Sauté it for 1 min.
  * Add soy sauce, vinegar, tomato sauce, 1 tsp chilli powder and a pinch of salt .Mix it and stir well for a minute.
  * Take 1 tbsp of corn flour and dissolve it in half cup of water. Pour it to the pan. Stir it for one more minute.
  * Add fried baby corns into the pan. Stir it gently, till each baby corns are well coated with the slurry.
  * Finally, Add two tbsp honey and roasted sesame seeds. Stir it well, so that honey gets coat with all the baby corns.
  * Garnish it with 1tbsp of chopped spring onions.

**Notes:**

  * You can add rice flour instead of corn flour.
