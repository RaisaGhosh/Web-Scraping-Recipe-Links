# Fish Fillet With Honey Lemon Butter Sauce

June 13, 2017August 26, 2021 Jainey

Post Views: 45,326

Credits @Maariaaana

#### The Honey Lemon Butter Sauce is just the perfect sauce to match any of
your baked fish fillet like salmon, cream dory, white fish or cod.

## Fish Fillet With Honey Lemon Butter Sauce

#### **INGREDIENTS:**

2 ½ tbsp honey  
3 ½ tbsp butter, softened  
3 ⅓ tbsp lemon juice  
1 tbsp lemon zest, finely chopped  
170g (6 oz.) fish fillets (like salmon, cream dory, white fish, snapper)  
1 Lemon, sliced  
fresh parsley, chopped (garnish)  
salt and pepper, to taste

#### **INSTRUCTIONS:**

  1. In a small bowl, stir honey, butter, lemon juice, and lemon zest until smooth. Chill briefly in the fridge. 
  2. Arrange fish fillets on a greased sheet pans; lightly season with sea salt and pepper.
  3. Bake fish at 500°F/260°C until opaque and just firm, about 10-15 minutes. Keep warm.
  4. To serve, top each portion with a tablespoon Honey-Lemon Butter sauce. 
  5. Garnish with chopped fresh herbs. 
  6. Serve and enjoy!
