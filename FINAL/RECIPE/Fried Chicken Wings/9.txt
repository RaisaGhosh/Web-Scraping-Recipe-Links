# KFC Style Chicken Wings Recipe

  * Shruti Bhatnagar 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method
  * Notes

How To Make KFC Style Chicken Wings

 **About KFC Style Chicken Wings Recipe** : Savour the popular fried chicken
from KFC, right inside your home with this quick and easy chicken wings
recipe! Spicy, zesty, simple and quick, this fried chicken wings recipe is
perfect for a movie or game night at home. Pair it with your favourite dip.

  * Total Cook Time 50 mins 

  * Prep Time 35 mins 

  * Cook Time 15 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of KFC Style Chicken Wings**

  *  **For marination:**
  * chicken wings (cleaned)
  * turmeric powder
  * curd
  * red chilli powder
  * ginger-garlic paste
  * black pepper
  * chicken masala
  * lemon
  *  **For coating**
  * masala chips
  * oil

## How to Make KFC Style Chicken Wings

HideShow Media

1.

Take the chicken wings in a bowl. Add all the ingredients or marination to it.

2.

Mix well and keep it in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.

3.

Meanwhile, take the packet of masala chips and dry roast it till slightly
brown. Crush the chips to powder.

4.

Now take the marinated chicken and coat each piece with the crushed chips.

5.

Heat oil in a wok and fry the coated chicken wings till crisp.

6.

Top the fried chicken wings with lemon juice and serve hot.

Key Ingredients: chicken wings (cleaned), turmeric powder, curd, red chilli
powder, ginger-garlic paste, black pepper, chicken masala, lemon, masala
chips, oil

#### Recipe Notes

You can take the quantity of ingredients as per the number of chicken wings
you wish to cook.
