# Honey, soy and sesame dipping sauce

  * _15m_ prep
  * _02m_ cook
  * makes _1/2 cup_

1

The Asian flavours in this dipping sauce are delicious paired with spring
rolls, dumplings or lamb cutlets. The sticky honey adds a touch of sweetness.

#### 8 Ingredients

  * 2 tsp vegetable oil 

  * 2 green onions, thinly sliced 

  * 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped 

  * 1 small red chilli, thinly sliced 

  * 1/3 cup soy sauce 

  * 1 tbsp honey 

  * 1 tsp sesame oil 

  * 1 tsp sesame seeds, toasted 

  * Select all ingredients 

Add to shopping list

#### 2 Method Steps

  * Step 1

Heat vegetable oil  (2 tsp vegetable oil) in a small frying pan over medium-
high heat. Add onion  (2 green onions, thinly sliced), garlic  (2 garlic
cloves, finely chopped) and chilli  (1 small red chilli, thinly sliced). Cook,
stirring, for 2 minutes or until onion and chilli starts to char. Transfer to
a heatproof bowl.

  * Step 2

Add soy sauce  (1/3 cup soy sauce), honey  (1 tbsp honey), sesame oil  (1 tsp
sesame oil), sesame seeds  (1 tsp sesame seeds, toasted) and 2 tablespoons
cold water. Season with pepper. Stir well to combine. Serve.
