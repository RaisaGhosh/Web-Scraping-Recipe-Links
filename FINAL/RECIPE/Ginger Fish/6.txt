## Ingredients for Ginger Fish Recipe

  * 0.13 kilogram fish fillets
  * 1/4 sprig spring onion, chopped finely
  * 1/2 tablespoon Oil
  * As required For Sauce
  * 3/4 teaspoon grated ginger
  * 1/2 flakes Garlic
  * 1/2 teaspoon Soy Sauce
  * 1/4 tablespoon Vinegar
  * 1/2 tablespoon Water
  * 1/4 teaspoon Sugar
  * 0.06 teaspoon Salt
  * 1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder
  * 1/2 teaspoon Lemon juice
