## Ingredients for Plain Ginger Fish Recipe

  * 1/4 pound Fish , cut into 1 inch cubes
  * 1/4  large onion
  * 3/4 tablespoon Soya sauce
  * 0.13 cup finely ground Ginger
  * 1/4 teaspoon Red chilli powder
  * 1  Green chillies, slit lengthwise
  * 0.06 teaspoon Cumin
  * 0.06 teaspoon Turmeric
  * 1/4 teaspoon finely cut coriander leaves
  * As required Salt
  * 1 tablespoon Oil For Frying
