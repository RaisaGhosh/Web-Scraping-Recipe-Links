# Schezwan Chicken Recipe

  * Chang 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Schezwan Chicken

Five spice coated boneless pieces of chicken stir fried with onions, ginger,
garlic and chillies. Served with steamed rice.

  * Total Cook Time1 hr 

  * Prep Time 15 mins 

  * Cook Time 45 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Schezwan Chicken**

  * 1/2 kg boneless chicken breasts
  * 4 Tbsp corn flour
  * 1 tsp salt
  * 1/2 tsp five spice powder
  * 75 ml oil
  * 7-8 dried red chillies
  * 2 tsp finely chopped garlic
  * 2 tsp finely chopped ginger
  * 6 spring onions - cut into 1' lengths
  * For the sauce:
  * 100 ml chicken stock
  * 2 tsp sugar
  * 1 Tbsp soya sauce
  * 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  * 1 tsp vinegar
  * 1 Tbsp Chinese wine/rum
  * 1/4 tsp five spice powder
  * 1/4 tsp black pepper
  * 2 tsp of corn flour - in 1 Tbsp stock/ water

## How to Make Schezwan Chicken

HideShow Media

1.

Coat chicken in corn flour, salt and five-spice powder. Brush off excess.

2.

Stir-fry the chicken until lightly colored. Remove from pan.

3.

Pour off all but 3 Tbsp of oil.

4.

Add chillies, garlic and ginger.

5.

Cook for 30 seconds before adding onions and all the sauce ingredients.

6.

Bring to a boil, return chicken to pan, heat through.

7.

Serve with steamed rice.
