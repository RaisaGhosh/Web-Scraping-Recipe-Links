# Lemon Chicken नींबू चिकन

Be the first person to review

Print

__Prep Time: 180 minutes __Cook Time: 25 minutes

__Total Time 180 minutes

Servings: 2

Cuisine: Chinese

### Ingredients

  * 1 tbsp olive oil/ vegetable oil
  * 450 gms chicken breast, cut into 1 inch cubes
  * 1 cup onion, chopped
  * 1 tbsp garlic, chopped
  * 1 tbsp ginger, chopped
  * ½ tsp lemon zest
  * 1 tsp freshly grounded black pepper
  * 1 tbsp soy sauce
  * 1 no lime juice

### Process

  * Heat oil in kadhai and add onion, garlic, ginger and saute until turn golden brown.
  * Now add chicken cubes. Sate continuously. Add soy sauce, lemon zest, ground black pepper. Squeeze lime juice and stir well.
  * When chicken is cooked remove and serve hot with rice. Sprinkle some green onion on top.
