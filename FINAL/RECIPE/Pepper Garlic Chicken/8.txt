# Garlic Butter Chicken Recipe

How To Make Garlic Butter Chicken

Bite-size chicken chunks are coated in a luscious dry gravy made with loads of
butter and garlic, the garlic butter chicken is the perfect flavorsome starter
that will have your friends and family savoring every bite of it.

  * Total Cook Time 30 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 20 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Garlic Butter Chicken**

  * 1/2 Kg Boneless chicken pieces 
  * 1 Egg
  * 2 tbsp Minced garlic 
  * 4 tbsp Butter
  * 1 small onion (chopped )
  * 1 tsp Black pepper powder 
  * Salt according to taste 
  * 1 tsp cornflour powder 
  * 2 tsp All-purpose flour (maida) 
  * Spring onion chopped (Optional)
  * Italian seasoning (optional)

## How to Make Garlic Butter Chicken

HideShow Media

1.

In a bowl add boneless chicken chunks, salt, half of the pepper, egg, and mix
well. Now add the maida and let it coat well. Mix and keep it aside for at
least 15 minutes.

2.

Fry the chicken chunks until golden brown on all sides.

3.

In another pan add butter and minced garlic, mix until the raw smell is gone.
Now add onions, red chili flakes, oregano, salt, and pepper and mix well.

4.

Make cornflour slurry with 1 tsp. cornflour and one bowl of water. Add the
slurry to the pan and mix well.

5.

Once the slurry is well cooked, add one bowl of water and make it liquidy
enough to coat all the chicken pieces.

6.

Add the fried chicken and mix until all pieces are coated with the gravy.
Garnish with spring onions in the end and serve hot.

Key Ingredients: Boneless chicken pieces , Egg, Minced garlic , Butter, onion
(chopped ), Black pepper powder , Salt according to taste , cornflour powder ,
All-purpose flour (maida) , Spring onion chopped (Optional), Italian seasoning
(optional)
