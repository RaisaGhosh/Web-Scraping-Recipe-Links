# Tom Yum Soup Recipe

  * Chef Jareuk Sri Arun, The ITC Marriott Hotel, New Delhi 

  * Recipe in Hindi
  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Tom Yum Soup

Fresh prawns, broccoli, baby corn, mushrooms in chicken and prawn stock.

  * Total Cook Time 35 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 25 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Tom Yum Soup**

  * 2 1/2 Cups Chicken stock
  * Prawn stock or Vegetable stock (optional)
  * 2 tbsp Lemon Grass, sliced
  * 1 tbsp Galangal/Thai ginger
  * 4-5 pieces Fresh prawns, medium
  * Broccoli and Baby corn (optional)
  * 3-4 pieces Mushrooms
  * 5-6 Thai Whole Chillies/bird eye
  * 8-10 Kafir Lime Leaves
  * 1 tbsp Lime Juice
  * to taste Fish sauce

## How to Make Tom Yum Soup

HideShow Media

1.

Take a deep pan, add the stock, lemon grass and galangal and bring it to a
boil.

2.

Then add the fresh prawns and mushrooms.

3.

Reduce the flame and stir gently.

4.

As the prawns start turning pink, add the Thai chillies and kafir lime leaves.

5.

Stir again and add the lime juice and fish sauce, which gives a salty flavour.

6.

Boil for 30 seconds and serve hot.

Key Ingredients: Chicken stock, Prawn stock or Vegetable stock (optional),
Lemon Grass, Galangal/Thai ginger, Fresh prawns, Broccoli and Baby corn
(optional), Mushrooms, Thai Whole Chillies/bird eye, Kafir Lime Leaves, Lime
Juice, Fish sauce
