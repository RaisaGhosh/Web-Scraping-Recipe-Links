# Crisp twice-fried lamb with Thai basil

By **Geoff Jansz**

  * Difficulty  
 **not too difficult**

  * Cooking time  
 **less than 30 minutes**

  * Serves  
 **serves 4**

##  Ingredients  Method

  * 1/3 cup sweet chilli sauce

  * ¼ cup oyster sauce

  * 2 tablespoons light soy sauce

  * 800g lamb strips

  * ¾ cup plain flour

  * vegetable oil, for deep frying

  * 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, extra

  * 1 small brown onion, sliced thinly

  * 2 cloves garlic, sliced thinly

  * 250g sugar snap peas, trimmed

  * 2 cups bean sprouts

  * 1 cup Thai basil leaves, loosely packed

##  Ingredients  Method

Combine sauces in small jug; pour two thirds of the sauce mixture into a
medium bowl with the lamb, mix well. Drain lamb, discard liquid.  
  
Coat lamb in flour; shake off excess. Heat oil in a wok; deep-fry lamb in
batches, until browned. Drain.  
  
Heat extra oil in cleaned wok; stir fry onion and garlic until onion softens.
Add peas and remaining sauce mixture; stir fry until peas are almost tender.  
  
Return lamb to wok; stir fry until hot. Remove from heat; stir in sprouts and
basil.
