# Prawn in XO Sauce Recipe

  *   * Sloppy Sticks 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How To Make Prawn in XO Sauce

 **About Prawn in XO Sauce Recipe** : A yummy prawn recipe to satiate your
taste buds! Tossed in a special XO sauce with dark soy and oyster sauce, this
recipe is a must try!

  * Total Cook Time 30 mins 

  * Prep Time 15 mins 

  * Cook Time 15 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Medium

###  **Ingredients of Prawn in XO Sauce**

  * 160 gms prawns (diced)
  * 5 gms garlic, chopped
  * 5 gms celery, chopped
  * 2 gms red chilli, chopped
  * 10 gms chilli paste
  * 5 ml dark soy paste
  * 5 gram potato starch
  * 10 ml refined oil
  * 5 gram salt
  * 6 gram chicken seasoning
  * 1 gram white pepper, chopped
  * 2 gram breakfast sugar
  * 10 gms spring onion, chopped
  * 10 ml XO sauce
  * 5 gram black fungus
  * 5 ml cooking wine
  * 5 ml oyster sauce

## How to Make Prawn in XO Sauce

HideShow Media

1.

Start the prepration by cutting the vegetables and get all the ingredents
available.

2.

Fry Prawn 13/15 In the refind oil. Add all cooking oil and veggies in the wok.

3.

Add all sauces and all seasoning in the wok.

4.

Garnish with the spring onion and fry garlic.

Key Ingredients: prawns (diced), garlic, celery, red chilli, chilli paste,
dark soy paste, potato starch, refined oil, salt, chicken seasoning, white
pepper, breakfast sugar, spring onion, XO sauce, black fungus, cooking wine,
oyster sauce
