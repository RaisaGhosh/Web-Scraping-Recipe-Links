# Chilli Prawns

_1.1k_ views

A popular Indo-Chinese appetizer dish and is a zingy treat for any day and any
occasion.

Jump to Recipe

Chilli Prawns are a very popular Indo-Chinese appetizer / snack dish.
Delicious and mouth-watering, these can be served as is or can be served with
fried rice too. Ideal for those weekend parties or sunday brunch.

5 from 1 vote

## Chilli Prawns Recipe

A popular Indo-Chinese appetizer dish and is a zingy treat for any day and any
occasion.

Prep Time15 mins

Cook Time13 mins

Course: Appetizer, Snack

Cuisine: Indo-Chinese

Keyword: indo-chinese

## Ingredients for Chilli Prawns

#### To Fry:

  * 1 tsp Soy Sauce – 1 tsp
  * 2 tblsp Corn Flour – 2 tblsp
  * 12 Prawns medium
  * Salt  to taste
  * Oil for deep frying

#### To Prepare Sauce:

  * 2 Spring Onions
  * 1/2 Capsicum
  * 1 inch Ginger
  * 4 Garlic Cloves
  * 1 Green Chilli
  * 3 tsp Soy Sauce
  * 3 tsp Red Chilli Sauce
  * 3 tsp Tomato Sauce
  * 1 tsp Corn Flour
  * 2 tsp Oil
  * Salt  to taste

## How to make Chilli Prawns

  * Clean and wash the prawns well.

  * Chop the spring onions and capsicum to medium size pieces.

  * Mix the corn flour along with water to form a paste.

  * Take a bowl, add the prawn pieces, salt, soy sauce, Corn flour and mix thoroughly.

  * Heat oil in a kadai for deep frying.

  * Fry the marinated prawn pieces in oil till crisp and golden brown.

  * To another pan, add oil and heat under medium flame.

  * Add the chopped ginger, garlic and green chilly.

  * Add the chopped spring onions and capsicum.

  * Add the soy sauce, tomato sauce, red chilly sauce and needed water.

  * Cook until it gets boiled well.

  * When they are cooked well, add the fried prawns and the corn flour paste and mix well.

  * Cook until the gravy has thickened and water is fully evaporated.

  * Sprinkle spring onion on top and serve hot.
