# Garlic Chilli Prawns Recipe

  * Malvika Singh 

  * Recipe in Hindi
  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Garlic Chilli Prawns

Succulent prawns splashed in olive oil and cooked with garlic and red
chillies.

  * Total Cook Time 25 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 15 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Garlic Chilli Prawns**

  * 4 large prawns
  * 3 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  * 7-8 garlic cloves, chopped
  * 3-4 red chillies, chopped
  * Salt, to taste

## How to Make Garlic Chilli Prawns

HideShow Media

1.

Clean the prawns and cut the arms and whiskers but keep the heads and shells
intact.

2.

Heat the oil in a pan and add the garlic and red chillies. Cook for about one
minute.

3.

Now add the prawns and stir for 30 seconds. Add the salt.

4.

Then leave them to cook for a minute and a half without covering.

5.

As they turn pink in color, switch off the gas. Serve hot.
