#### Schezwan Fried Rice

__

##### By Captain Ching

##### Preparation: 10 Min

##### Cooking: 10 Min

##### Difficulty: Easy

##### Serves: 4

# Introduction

Craving for some spicy Schezwan rice, but every door-step delivery seems run
off the mill? How about making Schezwan rice at home! With just a little bit
of rice and a sprinkling of Ching's Schezwan Fried Rice masala, you have a
dish that's as hot as it gets. Try it today!  

# Ingredients

  * 1 packet of Ching’s Secret Schezwan Fried Rice Masala

  * 4 tablespoons of oil

  * 2 cups of chopped vegetables of your choice (cabbage, carrot, capsicum, onion, french beans etc)

  * 6 cups of boiled rice

  * Chopped spring onion greens for garnishing

# STEP BY STEP METHOD

1\. In a pan, heat oil and stir-fry the chopped vegetables of your choice
(carrot, capsicum, onion, cabbage, french beans etc) for 2-3 minutes.

2\. Add boiled rice or leftover rice to the vegetables.

3\. Toss in the diced onions, capsicum, cauliflower, tomato, green peas and
rice. Give the mixture a good stir.

4\. Add Ching’s Secret Schezwan Masala.

5\. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes. Remove the fried rice in a bowl.

6\. Spicy hot Schezwan Fried Rice is ready. Garnish it with chopped spring
onion greens, serve hot and enjoy!
