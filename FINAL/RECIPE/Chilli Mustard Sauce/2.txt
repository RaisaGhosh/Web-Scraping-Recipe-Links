Vegetables in chilli mustard sauce is an innovative vegetable recipe of stir
frying vegetables in a chilli(green chilli) and mustard based paste. A spicy
and pungent vegetable preparation which is best served with steamed rice.

##  Vegetables In Chilli Mustard Sauce recipe - How to make Vegetables In
Chilli Mustard Sauce

## Tags

Sabzis, Curries

Preparation Time: -  &nbspCooking; Time: -  &nbspTotal; Time: 0 mins  
Show me for servings

Ingredients  

  
1/2 cup green peas (shellled)  
5 to 6 french beans  
1/2 small sized cauliflower  
1 medium sized capsicum  
1 medium sized carrot  
5 to 6 mushrooms (khumbh)  
2 tbsp oil  
3 green chillies  
2 spring onion whites  
1 tbsp mustard (rai / sarson) paste  
3 tbsp red chilli sauce  
1/2 cup fresh cream  
salt to taste  
1/4 tsp black pepper (kalimirch) powder  

Method

  
  

  1. Place peas with one cup of water in a microwave bowl. cook, uncovered, at microwave high (100%) for five minutes. drain and refresh in cold water. 
  2. String, wash and cut french beans into diamonds each side measuring half inch. wash and separate cauliflower into small florets. wash, de-seed and cut capsicum into half inch sized dices. peel, wash and slice carrot diagonally in half centimetre thick pieces. clean, wash and slice mushrooms thickly. remove stems, wash and finely chop green chillies
  3. Place cauliflower, french beans and carrot in a microwave bowl with one cup of water and cook, covered, on microwave high(100%) for eight minutes. remove, drain and keep aside. 
  4. In a deep microwave casserole, mix oil, green chillies, spring onions, capsicum and cook uncovered on microwave high (100%) for two minutes. 
  5. Add peas, cauliflower, french beans, carrots, mushrooms, mustard paste, chilli sauce and fresh cream. season with salt and pepper and cook, covered, on microwave high(100%) for two minutes. serve hot.
