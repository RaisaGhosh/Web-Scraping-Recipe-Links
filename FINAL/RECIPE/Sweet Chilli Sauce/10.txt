#  Easy sweet chilli sauce

by

Marcello Tully

  * Condiment
  * easy
  * 6 
  * 15 minutes

PT15M

Whip up this easy sweet chilli dipping sauce recipe from Marcello Tully and
serve it alongside some spring rolls or fish cakes.

#### Easy sweet chilli sauce

  * 1  red chilli, finely chopped
  * 25ml of water
  * 1 pinch of  salt
  * 85ml of white wine vinegar
  * 175g of caster sugar
  * 12g of  fish sauce
  * 1/2  garlic clove
  * 1 tsp  fresh ginger, finely chopped
  * 1 pinch of cornflour

shopping List

##  Method

print recipe

1

To make the dip, mix the water and cornflour together to dilute the cornflour.
Place all the remaining ingredients in a saucepan and bring to the boil

  * 25ml of water
  * 1 pinch of salt
  * 85ml of white wine vinegar
  * 175g of caster sugar
  * 12g of fish sauce
  * 1/2 garlic clove
  * 1 tsp fresh ginger, finely chopped
  * 1 pinch of cornflour
  * 1 red chilli, finely chopped

2

Once boiling, whisk in the water and cornflour solution and boil for another
1-2 minutes, then remove from the heat and leave to cool

3

Serve in small bowls as a dip alongside your favourite Southeast Asian dishes
