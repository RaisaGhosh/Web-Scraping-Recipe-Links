# Peanut Sauce Recipe

  * Food Blogger Divya Burman 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Peanut Sauce

 **About Peanut Sauce Recipe** : A very quick and easy accompaniment, peanut
sauce is a tasty one to pair with a number of different dishes. Serve this
yummy and easy to make peanut sauce in just 20 minutes. Best and popularly
served with chicken satay.

  * Total Cook Time 25 mins 

  * Prep Time 05 mins 

  * Cook Time 20 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Peanut Sauce**

  * 3 Tbsp peanuts with skin, crushed in a mortar and pestle, roasted
  * 1 cup coconut milk
  * 3/4 tbsp palm sugar
  * 1 tsp red curry paste
  * to taste salt

## How to Make Peanut Sauce

HideShow Media

1.

Heat coconut milk.

2.

Add smashed peanuts and boil.

3.

Add palm sugar followed by curry paste and mix well.

4.

Season with salt and taste.

5.

Sauce will thicken as it cools.

Key Ingredients: peanuts with skin, crushed in a mortar and pestle, coconut
milk, palm sugar, red curry paste, salt
