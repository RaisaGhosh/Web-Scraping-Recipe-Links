# Devil's Pork Curry

  * Easy
  * 0:45 Prep
  * 4 Servings 

  *   * Save Recipe

by Pamella

a chance to win. Learn more.

Upload your recipe photo

A Vindaloo style curry. We are a curry loving household and this is my current
favourite. I buy a boneless roast and dice it up, crackle and all. I also
always double this recipe. \- Pamella

## Ingredients

  * 750 g diced pork

  * 1 tablespoon white vinegar

  * 2 tablespoons light soy sauce

  * 2 tablespoons oil

  * 2 onions, sliced

  * 1 tablespoon chopped ginger

  * 2 cloves garlic

  * 12 dried curry leaves

  * 1 teaspoon brown sugar

  * 1 cinnamon stick

  * 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds

  * 4 small red chillies, chopped

  * 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric

  * 1 tablespoon chopped fresh lemon grass

  * 1 teaspoon tamarind concentrate

  * 2 teaspoons fish sauce

  * 1 cup water

  * 1 small beef stock cube, crumbled

  * 1/3 cup macadamia nuts, finely chopped (optional)

## Method

  1. Combine pork, vinegar and soy sauce. Mix well. Stand for a minimum of 20 minutes. Drain pork and reserve liquid. 

  2. Heat oil. Stir in next 10 ingredients. Saute for approximately 5 minutes. 

  3. Add pork. Stir until browned. 

  4. Combine remaining ingredients including reserved liquid. Add to pork mix. Bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 15 minutes. 

  5. Remove lid, simmer for 30 minutes or until liquid has reduced. Remove cinnamon and serve.
