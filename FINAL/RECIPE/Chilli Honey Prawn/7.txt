## Ingredients for Honey Chilli Prawns Recipe

  * 125 gram Prawns
  * 1.50 tablespoon Oil
  * 1/2 tablespoon ginger garlic crushed
  * 1/4 teaspoon Red Chili flakes
  * 1/2  Medium Sized Onion Sliced
  * 1/2 teaspoon Soy sauce
  * 1/2 tablespoon Red chili sauce
  * 1/4 teaspoon honey
  * 1/4 teaspoon Cornstarch
  * As required Salt
  * As required fresh coriander leaves chopped
  * 2 tablespoon Water
  * 1  Dry red chilies de seeded
