#  Honey Chilli Prawns

  

### ****

### **Ingredients:**

  * 1kg of fresh prawns, peeled with the tails left on and deveined
  * 3tbls Honey Australiaâ€™s Chilli Honey
  * 4 cloves of garlic, crushed
  * 3tbls of olive oil

### Method:

Mix together the chilli honey, garlic and olive oil, until well combined.  
Pour mixture over prawns and let them marinade for about 30minuted in the
refrigerator.

Heat wok or BBQ on high and stir fry the prawns until cooked and caramelised.  
Serve with a light squeeze of fresh lime juice (optional) and enjoy!
