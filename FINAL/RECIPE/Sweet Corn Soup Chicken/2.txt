# Chicken Sweet Corn Soup Recipe

  * Niru Gupta

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Chicken Sweet Corn Soup

An old favorite is always a hit at family meals.

  * Total Cook Time 45 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 35 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Chicken Sweet Corn Soup**

  * 1 tin sweet corn-cream style*
  * 4 cups chicken stock
  * 2 Tbsp cornflour
  * 1 1/2 tsp salt
  * 1 egg, slightly beaten
  * 1/2 cup cooked diced chicken
  * Vinegar 1 tbsp

## How to Make Chicken Sweet Corn Soup

HideShow Media

1.

Dissolve the cornflour in 1/2 cup water and keep aside.

2.

Empty the contents of the corn tin into a saucepan, add the stock and bring to
a boil.

3.

Add the cornflour solution and let simmer for about 10 minutes.

4.

Add the salt and the shredded chicken and keep on simmer.

5.

Pour the egg in a thin stream over the simmering soup, stirring slowly all the
time.

6.

Add the vinegar and serve hot.

7.

*Alternatively, you can crush some boiled corn, add enough water to make it into a thick, dough like consistency and use.
