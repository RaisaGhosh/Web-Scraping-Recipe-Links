#### 7 Ingredients

  * 1 litre chicken stock 

  * 1 tbsp soy sauce 

  * 1 tsp finely grated ginger 

  * 310g can corn kernels 

  * 310g can creamed corn 

  * 3 small chicken breast fillets, thinly sliced 

  * 3 green onions, chopped 

  * Select all ingredients 

Add to shopping list

#### 4 Method Steps

  * Step 1

Place the chicken stock  (1 litre chicken stock) in a large saucepan with the
soy sauce  (1 tbsp soy sauce), ginger  (1 tsp finely grated ginger), corn
kernels  (310g can corn kernels) and creamed corn  (310g can creamed corn).

  * Step 2

Cook over medium heat and simmer for 5 minutes.

  * Step 3

Add the chicken  (3 small chicken breast fillets, thinly sliced) and green
onions  (3 green onions, chopped) and cook for 3-4 minutes or until chicken is
cooked through.

  * Step 4

Serve sprinkled with extra chopped green onion.

### Recipe Notes

There are many variations of this perennial family favourite. This is a very
quick and easy version you can make from pantry staples and store-bought
stock. You can also add noodles or extra green vegetables to this recipe and
use salt-reduced stock for a healthier meal.
