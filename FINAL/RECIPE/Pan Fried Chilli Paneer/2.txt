# Pan Fried Chilli Paneer Recipe

  * Ram Bahadur Budhathoki 

  * Chowman 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How To Make Pan Fried Chilli Paneer

Pan Fried Chilli Paneer Recipe**: Diced marinated paneer, sautéed with fresh
vegetables, tossed in oyster sauce; this one is a heavenly treat you just
can't miss!

  * Total Cook Time 30 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 20 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Pan Fried Chilli Paneer**

  * 1 Large paneer cube (diced)
  * Shredded vegetables including capsicums, onion & carrots
  * Broth powder
  * Chilli paste
  * Oyster sauce
  * Red wine
  * Sesame oil

## How to Make Pan Fried Chilli Paneer

HideShow Media

1.

Sautee the chopped celery and veggies, along with the diced paneer cubes in a
frying pan.

2.

Add some salt and broth powder, squeeze in some chilli paste, oyster sauce
with few drops of red wine for the luxury aroma.

3.

Drop a few spoons of sesame oil and do the final garnishing with spring
onions.

Key Ingredients: paneer cube (diced), Shredded vegetables including capsicums,
onion & carrots, Broth powder, Chilli paste, Oyster sauce, Red wine, Sesame
oil
