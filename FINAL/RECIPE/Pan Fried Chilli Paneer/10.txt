# Chinese-style Chilli Paneer recipe

## ​Ingredients required

400 grams paneer, 3 tbsp cornflour, ½ onion, ½ capsicum, ¼ yellow bell pepper,
¼ red bell pepper, 1 tbsp chopped garlic and 3 tbsp chopped spring onions.

istock

## ​Spices and sauces required

1 tsp red chilli powder, 1 tbsp chilli sauce, 2 tbsp soya sauce, 2 tbsp tomato
ketchup, ½ tbsp vinegar and salt as per taste.

istock

## Step 1

Add cubed paneer to a bowl. Sprinkle some salt, red chilli powder and 2 tbsp
cornflour on it. Mix well to coat the paneer cubes properly.

istock

## ​Step 2

Heat oil in a kadhai for shallow-frying and drop the coated paneer cubes one
by one in it. Fry the paneer cubes until they turn golden brown in colour.

istock

## ​Step 3

Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan. Add chopped garlic and cubed onions. Saute them for
a minute. Now add cubed capsicum, yellow and bell pepper. Toss them for
another minute.

istock

## Step 4

Now add chilli sauce, soya sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar and gently toss the
ingredients on high heat to coat them well with the sauces.

pexels

## ​Step 5

Add a dash of water now and adjust the salt as per your taste. Now add the
fried paneer cubes to the pan and toss them a bit. You can add some extra
water if you want more gravy.

istock

## ​Step 6

Now mix 1 tbsp cornflour with 2 tbsp water to make a slurry. Add this slurry
to the pan and give a nice mix. Cook for just two more minutes and then turn
off the flame.

istock

## ​Ready to be served

Once done, take out the Chilli Paneer in a serving bowl, garnish with chopped
spring onions and serve.
