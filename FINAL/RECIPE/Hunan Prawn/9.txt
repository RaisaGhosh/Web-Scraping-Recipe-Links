# About Hunan Shrimp Recipe:

This hot and spicy hunan shrimp is a combination of tender shrimp that have
been wok fried then coated in a flavorful hunan sauce Serve up your hunan
shrimp with stir fried vegetables and rice for a light and healthy family meal
Even better, you can make this 20 minute dinner in less time than it takes to
run to your local chinese food restaurant.
