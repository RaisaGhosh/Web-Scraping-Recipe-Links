#### 6 Ingredients

  * 6-8 flathead fillets 

  * 4 tbsp butter 

  * 3 tbsp olive oil 

  * 2 cloves garlic 

  * 3/4 cup plain flour 

  * Salt & pepper, to season 

  * Select all ingredients 

Add to shopping list

#### 3 Method Steps

  * Step 1

Place plain flour in a large bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Coat cleaned
and dried fish in flour.

  * Step 2

In a frypan, combine butter, oil and garlic, over low-medium heat until
melted.

  * Step 3

Increase heat to medium and add fish to frypan. Cook for 1-2 minutes each
side. Serve with salad or steamed vegetables.
