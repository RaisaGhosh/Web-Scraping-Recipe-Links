How to make Chicken Ramen

Here we bring you one easy recipe to make ramen at home without any fuss.

  * Total Cook Time 30 mins 

  * Prep Time 15 mins 

  * Cook Time 15 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Chicken Ramen**

  * 1 Tbsp Butter
  * 1 cup Corn
  * 2 Eggs (Soft Boiled)
  * 2 cups Fresh Spinach
  * 4 cups Chicken Stock
  * 2-3 Crushed Garlic
  * 2 Inches Ginger (Sliced)
  * 2 Chicken Breast (Shredded)
  * 2 Servings Cooked Ramen Noodles
  * 1 tsp Soya Sauce

## How to Make Chicken Ramen

HideShow Media

1.

Heat butter in a saucepan, fry corn until warm. Set aside. Soft-boil 2 eggs
and set aside. Blanch the spinach and set aside.

2.

Bring chicken stock, garlic, ginger and scallions to a boil. Boil for 30
minutes.

3.

Add the chicken, simmer for 20 minutes. Remove, and set aside.

4.

Add the soy sauce to the broth.

5.

Shred the chicken.

6.

Put everything together. Ramen is ready!

Key Ingredients: Butter, Corn, Eggs (Soft Boiled), Fresh Spinach, Chicken
Stock, Crushed Garlic, Ginger (Sliced), Chicken Breast (Shredded), Cooked
Ramen Noodles, Soya Sauce
