# Chicken lung fung soup

Preparation Time: 10 Minutes | Cooking Time: 15 Minutes | Servings: 5

__________  

#### **Ingredients:**

Oil- 1/2 tsp  
Ginger - 1 tsp (Finely chopped)  
Garlic - 1 tsp (Finely chopped)  
Green Chili - 1/2 tsp (Finely chopped)  
Minced Chicken- 1/2 cup (250gm)  
Salt - to taste  
Water - 250ml  
Light Soy Sauce - 1 Tbsp  
Black Pepper - as required (Crushed)  
Egg whites -2  
Corn flour - 1 Tbsp  
Water - 2tbsp  
Coriander Leaves- for garnishing

####  **Steps:**

  1. Connect the power cord to ROBOCOOK and switch 'ON' button. Press the Sauté Button in front dashboard and wait for 30 secs for the inner cooking pot to get heated up.
  2. Add oil to the inner cooking pot and let it heat a bit. Add Ginger, Garlic, Green Chilli and saute for 2 minutes
  3. Next add Minced chicken, salt and continue to saute for 2 minutes. Add soya sauce, pepper and mix well. Let it open cook for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Now mix Corn flour with water together and add to the pot. Allow it to boil for 2-3 minutes
  5. Add the egg (beaten), coriander leaves and give it a stir. Let it open cook for 2 minutes. Switch to keep warm or off mode.
  6. Serve the Chicken Lung Fung Soup with bread.
