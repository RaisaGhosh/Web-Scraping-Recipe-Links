# Honey Chicken Recipe

  * Review

How to make Honey Chicken

This recipe has a sweet and sour taste that will impress your friends and
family in no time. Besides being delectable, this recipe is also very quick
and easy.

  * Total Cook Time 25 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 15 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Honey Chicken**

  * 250 gms Boneless Chicken 
  * 2 tbsp Honey
  * 1 tbsp Butter
  * to taste Salt
  * to taste Black Pepper
  * 1 tsp Ginger Garlic paste

## How to Make Honey Chicken

HideShow Media

1.

Firstly, take boneless chicken pieces and fry them for 4-5 minutes on medium
flame. Once done, keep them aside.

2.

Then place a pan on medium flame, add butter and honey onto it. Once the
butter is melted, turn off the flame.

3.

Now add lemon juice, ginger garlic paste and salt in the same pan and mix
everything well.

4.

Add the chicken pieces into this honey mixture and cook for about 10 minutes
on low-medium flame. Ps: If you want a fiery texture, you can also use
schezwan sauce.

5.

Your honey chicken recipe is ready to be savoured.

Key Ingredients: Boneless Chicken , Honey, Butter, Salt, Black Pepper, Ginger
Garlic paste
