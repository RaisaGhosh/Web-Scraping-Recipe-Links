### Fish in Chilli Garlic Sauce

Recipe by

###  Chandra Bhat

Total Time:  
15-30 minutes

Serves: 4

Nutrition facts:

240 calories,  9 grams fat

Category: Non-Vegetarian Recipe

Course: Starter Recipe

Technique: Microwave Recipe

Difficulty: Medium

### Ingredients

  * 1/4 kg - thinly cut fish slices (bone less) 
  * 2 tbsp - lime juice 
  * 1/2 tsp - freshly ground black pepper 
  * 1/4 cup - spring onions, chopped 
  * 1/4 cup - mushrooms, chopped 
  * 2 green chillies, minced 
  * 1 tsp - ginger, mince 
  * 1 tsp - soya sauce 
  * salt to taste 
  * For Sauce: 
  * 2 tbsp - oil 
  * 1/2 cup - onions, minced 
  * 1 tbsp - garlic, minced 
  * 1 tsp - kashmiri chilli powder1 tbsp - vinegar 
  * 2 tbsp - tomato ketchup 
  * 1 tsp - sugar 
  * 1/2 tsp - MSG, optional 
  * 1/2 cup - water 
  * 2 tsp - corn flour 
  * salt to taste

# How to Make Fish in Chilli Garlic Sauce

  * Wash the fish and pat dry. Sprinkle with lime juice and pepper and keep aside for 15 minutes. 
  * Combine rest of the ingredients together. Spread 1/2 of them in a shallow glass dish. 
  * Place the fish slices over them. Sprinkle remaining mixture on top. 
  * Cover and cook on high for 4-5 minutes. 
  * Meanwhile prepare the sauce on the gas burner. 
  * For Sauce: 
  * Mix chili powder with vinegar. 
  * Heat oil in a fry pan. Fry garlic till brown. Add onions fry till brown. 
  * Add the chili paste and fry for 1/2 minute. Add salt and tomato ketchup. 
  * Mix corn flour with water and add to the pan. Add salt, sugar and MSG (optional) 
  * Cook stirring till the sauce thickens. 
  * When the fish is cooked pour the hot sauce over it. 
  * Cook on full power covered for 2 more minutes. Serve hot with fried rice or noodles.
