# Fish with mandarin and dill sauce

  * _10m_ prep
  * _20m_ cook
  * _4_ servings

#### 6 Ingredients

  * 500g straight cut frozen chips 

  * 50g butter 

  * 500g barramundi or other firm white fish, cut into 4 fillets 

  * 2 tbsp dill, snipped 

  * 4 Imperial mandarins 

  * Salad leaves, to serve 

  * Select all ingredients 

Add to shopping list

#### 4 Method Steps

  * Step 1

Preheat oven to 210C or 190C fan-forced. Spread chips in a single layer on a
baking tray. Cook according to packet instructions.

  * Step 2

Finely grate rind of 1 mandarin. Juice 2 mandarins and set aside. Peel and
segment remaining 2 mandarins and set aside.

  * Step 3

Melt butter in a non-stick frying pan on medium. Add rind and swirl to
combine. Cook fish for 1-2 mins each side until golden and cooked through.
Drain on kitchen paper.

  * Step 4

Reheat butter in pan. Add juice and simmer for 30 secs. Add mandarin segments
and dill, swirl to warm through. Top fish with mandarin and dill sauce. Serve
with chips with salad leaves.
