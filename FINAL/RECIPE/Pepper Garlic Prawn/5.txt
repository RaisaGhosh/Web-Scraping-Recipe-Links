# Garlic Prawns Recipe

  * Divya Burman, Food Blogger 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method
  * Notes

How to make Garlic PrawnsNDTV Food

Marinated in a spicy garlic sauce, this easy to cook prawn recipe is a great
pick for a quick bite at your dinner party.

  * Total Cook Time1 hr 

  * Prep Time 45 mins 

  * Cook Time 15 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Medium

###  **Ingredients of Garlic Prawns**

  * 1 Kg Prawns (deshelled, deveined with tails intact), medium
  *  **For the marination:**
  * 1 tbsp Garlic and Green chillis blended paste
  * 1/2 tsp Black pepper (crushed)
  * 3 tbsp Lemon Juice
  * 1/2 tsp Chilli sauce
  * 7 small Garlic cloves, chopped
  * 2 tsp Cornflour
  * 7 tbsp Soy sauce
  * 1/2 tbsp Red chilli powder
  *  **For the sauce:**
  * 2 tbsp White vinegar
  * 3 tbsp Soy sauce
  * 1 tsp Sesame seeds, roasted
  * 2 tsp Green chillies , chopped

## How to Make Garlic Prawns

HideShow Media

1.

Marinate prawns and leave aside for half an hour.

2.

In a non stick pan. Put 2 tablespoon sesame oil, when hot add prawns.

3.

Stir fry till prawn tails are curled and cooked(do not overcook).

4.

Put in serving dish and serve with sauce.

Key Ingredients: Prawns (deshelled, deveined with tails intact), Garlic and
Green chillis blended paste, Black pepper (crushed), Lemon Juice, Chilli
sauce, Garlic cloves, Cornflour, Soy sauce, Red chilli powder, White vinegar,
Soy sauce, Sesame seeds, Green chillies

#### Recipe Notes

You can use butter instead of oil for a more enhanced taste of Garlic Prawns.
