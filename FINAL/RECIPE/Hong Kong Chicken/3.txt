# HongKong Chicken

__Print Recipe

(3.8 / 5)

  * Cuisine: Chinese
  * Course: Starter
  * Skill Level: Easy

Hongkong Chicken is a easy to make starter from Chinese Cuisine.

### Ingredients

  * Add to Shopping List + Chicken - 500 gram 
  * Add to Shopping List + Chilly sauce - 1/2 tspn 
  * Add to Shopping List + Green chilly - 20 no 
  * Add to Shopping List + Worcestershire sauce - 1/2 tspn 
  * Add to Shopping List + Tomato sauce - 1 tspn 
  * Add to Shopping List + Soya sauce - 1/2 tspn 
  * Add to Shopping List + Red food colour - 1 small pinch 
  * Add to Shopping List + Salt - to taste 
  * Add to Shopping List + Oil for frying

### Step by step method

  * ### Step 1

Marinate chicken pieces with salt for 10 minutes.

  * ### Step 2

Heat oil in a pan, Deep fry the chicken pieces and keep it aside.

  * ### Step 3

Heat 2 tspn oil in a pan, Add slitted green chillies to it and saute well. Add
food colour and mix it.

  * ### Step 4

Add all sauces one by one and mix everything well.

  * ### Step 5

To this add fried chicken pieces and mix well. Stir everything for some
minutes. Serve hot.
