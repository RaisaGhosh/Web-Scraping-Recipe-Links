# Vegetable Thai Red Curry Recipe

  * Niru Gupta

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Vegetable Thai Red Curry

 **About Vegetable Thai Red Curry Recipe** : Red bell pepper, broccoli,
cauliflower, snow peas, zucchini and bamboo shoots cooked in distinct Thai
flavours.

  * Total Cook Time 40 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 30 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Vegetable Thai Red Curry**

  * Fresh Vegetables (red bell pepper, broccoli, cauliflower, snow peas, zucchini and bamboo shoots) - simmered in an aromatic coconut milk broth.
  * 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  * 1 tbsp Thai Red Curry Paste
  * 1 Sweet Red Bell Pepper, julienne
  * 1 small Broccoli, chopped
  * 1/4 Cauliflower, chopped
  * 2 tbsp Unsweetened Coconut Milk
  * 1 small Zucchini, julienne
  * 2 tbsp Bamboo Shoots, julienne

## How to Make Vegetable Thai Red Curry

HideShow Media

1.

Heat oil in a large soup pot or sauce pan over medium high heat.

2.

Add curry paste, mashing with the back of a spoon, and saute until fragrant,
about one minute.

3.

Add broccoli and cauliflower and mix well to coat with curry paste.

4.

Stir-fry for two minutes.

5.

Add bell pepper and stir-fry for two minutes.

6.

Add coconut milk and stir.

7.

Bring to a boil and then turn down heat to let simmer for 10 minutes, stirring
occasionally.

8.

Add zucchini and bamboo shoots and simmer for five more minutes. Stir gently.

Key Ingredients: Fresh Vegetables (red bell pepper, broccoli, cauliflower,
snow peas, zucchini and bamboo shoots) - simmered in an aromatic coconut milk
broth., Vegetable Oil, Thai Red Curry Paste, Sweet Red Bell Pepper, Broccoli,
Cauliflower, Unsweetened Coconut Milk, Zucchini, Bamboo Shoots
