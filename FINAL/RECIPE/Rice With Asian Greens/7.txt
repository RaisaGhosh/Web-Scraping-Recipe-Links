# Ginger chilli fish with herbed rice and Asian greens

  * _15m_ prep
  * _10m_ cook
  * _4_ servings

#### 12 Ingredients

  * 250g (1 1/4 cups) white long-grain rice

  * Canola oil spray 

  * 4 (about 150g each) white fish fillets 

  * 2 tbsp sushi seasoning 

  * 2 tbsp salt-reduced soy sauce 

  * 5cm-piece fresh ginger, peeled, cut into matchsticks 

  * 1 long red chilli, seeded, thinly sliced 

  * 200g snow peas, halved diagonally lengthways 

  * 1 bunch choy sum, trimmed, leaves separated 

  * 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander 

  * 2 tbsp chopped fresh mint 

  * Fresh mint leaves, to serve

#### 3 Method Steps

  * Step 1

Cook rice following packet directions. Meanwhile, heat a large non-stick
frying pan over medium-high heat. Spray with oil. Cook fish for 2-3 minutes
each side or until cooked through. Transfer to a plate. Add sushi seasoning,
soy sauce, ginger and chilli to pan. Cook for 1 minute or until mixture is
heated through and slightly reduced.

  * Step 2

Meanwhile, cook snow peas and choy sum in a saucepan of boiling water until
bright green and tender-crisp. Drain. Add coriander and chopped mint to rice
and stir to combine. Top with mint leaves.

  * Step 3

Divide snow peas, choy sum and fish among plates. Drizzle with ginger mixture
and serve with rice.
