# Grilled prawns with chilli sauce, tomato salsa, lime mayo and flatbreads

Serves **4**

20 May 2015

20 May 2015

#### Recipe by Yellow Team

Add to my Recipes

Advertisement

  * Steps
  * Ingredients

## Ingredients

### Chilli sauce

  * 1 tsp olive oil
  * 2 jalepeno chillies, sliced
  * 2 long red chillies, sliced
  * ½ red onion, chopped
  * 400g tinned crushed tomatoes
  * ¼ cup white wine vinegar
  * salt and pepper to taste

### Tomato salsa

  * 8 cherry tomatoes, diced
  * ½ red onion, finely chopped
  * ½ long red chilli, finely chopped
  * ½ cup chopped coriander
  * juice of 1 lime
  * salt and pepper

### Lime Mayonnaise

  * 1 egg yolk
  * juice of 1 lime
  * 2/3 cup grapeseed oil
  * salt and pepper to taste

### Yoghurt flatbreads

  * 1 cup self raising flour
  * 1 cup plain yoghurt
  * 1 tsp salt

### Prawns

  * 16 prawns, heads and shells removed, deveined
  * ½ red chilli
  * 1 tbsp olive oil
  * salt and pepper

## Steps

 **For the chilli sauce**

  * Add 1 teaspoon oil to small saucepan over medium heat. Add jalepeno and long red chillies and red onion and cook for 2 minutes. Add tinned tomatoes and vinegar and simmer for 20 -25 minutes until thickened. Season and blitz with a stick blender. Set aside to cool.

**For the salsa**

  * Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and mix gently. Set aside.

**For the lime mayonnaise**

  * Combine egg yolk and lime juice in a small bowl and blitz with a stick blender until slightly thickened. Add oil in a thin stream while processing until thick. Season and set aside in fridge.

**For the flatbreads**

  * Combine flour, yoghurt and salt in a bowl and mix until a soft dough forms. Wrap in cling film and set aside for 30 minutes. Divide dough into 8 pieces and roll out thinly. Fry on a lightly oiled griddle pan over high heat until browned and cooked. Cover with clean tea towel to keep warm.
  * Place prawns in a small bowl, add chilli, oil and seasoning . Cook prawns in a frying pan over a high heat for 2 minutes on each side or until cooked through.

**To serve**

  * Divide chilli sauce between 4 serving plates. Top with prawns, salsa and lime mayonnaise. Serve with flatbreads.
