#### 8 Ingredients

  * 1/3 cup cornflour 

  * 1 tbsp sea salt 

  * 1 tbsp cracked black pepper 

  * 750g green king prawns, peeled, tails intact, deveined 

  * 1/4 cup vegetable oil 

  * 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves 

  * 1 long red chilli, thinly sliced 

  * 1 green onion, thinly sliced 

  * Select all ingredients 

Add to shopping list

#### 3 Method Steps

  * Step 1

Combine cornflour  (1/3 cup cornflour), salt  (1 tbsp sea salt) and pepper  (1
tbsp cracked black pepper) in a large bowl. Add half the prawns  (750g green
king prawns, peeled, tails intact, deveined). Toss to coat, shaking off
excess.

  * Step 2

Heat half the oil  (1/4 cup vegetable oil) in a large, non-stick frying pan
over high heat. Cook prawns, stirring, for 3 to 4 minutes or until pink.
Repeat with remaining cornflour mixture, prawns and oil.

  * Step 3

Place prawns on a plate. Top with coriander  (1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves),
chilli  (1 long red chilli, thinly sliced) and onion  (1 green onion, thinly
sliced). Serve.

### Recipe Notes

Serves 6 as a starter.

Coat prawns in cornflour mixture just before cooking to avoid coating becoming
soggy.
