### Red chilli and tamarind chutney

Recipe by

###  Rajeswari Vijayanand

15-30 minutes

Serves: 4

Nutrition facts:

### Ingredients

  * 2 to 3 - red chillies 
  * 1/2 tbsp - tamarind, tightly packed 
  * Salt as required 
  * 1 tbsp - onion, chopped 
  * To temper: 
  * 1 tsp - oil 
  * 1/2 tsp - mustard 
  * 1/2 tsp - urad dal 
  * A few - curry leaves

# How to Make Red chilli and tamarind chutney

  * Grind into a smooth paste, red chillies, tamarind and salt with water. 
  * Transfer to a bowl. Temper with the the tempering ingredients and add onion. 
  * Fry till golden brown and mix it to the chutney. 
  * Mix well and enjoy as an accompaniment with idly.
