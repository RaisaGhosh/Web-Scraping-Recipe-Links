Round up your meal with this lip-smacking dessert. Crispy fried noodles
drizzled with honey is a hot and cold combination when served with a nice
dollop of vanilla ice-cream. Even your kids will love the noodles.
Traditionally this dish is called "darsaan".

##  Honeyed Noodles with Vanilla Ice-cream recipe - How to make Honeyed
Noodles with Vanilla Ice-cream

## Tags

Chinese DessertsQuick NoodlesQuick Stir-FriesKids Noodles

Preparation Time: 15 mins  &nbspCooking; Time: 15 mins  &nbspTotal; Time: 30
mins     4Makes 4 servings  
Show me for servings

Ingredients  

  
For The Noodles  
4 samosa pattis , cut into thin strips  
oil for deep-frying  
  
For The Honey Sauce  
1 tbsp sugar  
1 tbsp honey  
1 tsp sesame seeds (til)  
  
For Serving  
vanilla ice-cream  

Method

  
For the noodles  

For the noodles

  1. Heat the oil in a broad non-stick pan and add the samosa patti strips and fry on a medium flame for 2 to 3 minutes or till they turn brown in colour.
  2. Drain on absorbent paper and keep aside.

  
For the honey sauce  

For the honey sauce

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of water in a broad non-stick pan , add the sugar and cook on a medium flame for 1 minutes, while stirring occasionally.
  2. Add the honey and sesame seeds, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 1 minute, while stirring occasionally.

  
How to proceed  

How to proceed

  1. Transfer the noodles on a serving plate, pour the honey sauce immediately over them and serve with vanilla ice-cream.
