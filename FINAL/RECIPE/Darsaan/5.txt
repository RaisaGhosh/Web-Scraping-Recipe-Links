## ingredients

Nutrition

  * 120  g flattened egg noodles or 120 g wonton skins, cut into strips 
  * oil (for frying)
  * 4  tablespoons water
  * 50  g caster sugar
  * 8  teaspoons honey
  * 4  tablespoons orange squash (optional)
  * 1  tablespoon Kahlua (optional)
  * 2  teaspoons lightly roasted sesame seeds or 2 teaspoons slivered almonds (to garnish)

## directions

  * Heat oil in a wok and deep fry the noodles/ wanton strips till crisp.
  * Heat a heavy bottomed pan make a single thread syrup of the castor sugar with 60 ml.
  * water.
  * Add the orange squash and honey and toss the fried noodles/ wanton crips in it to coat.
  * Serve hot in 4 bowls and sprinkle 1/4 tsp sesame seeds/ slivered almonds in each bowl, and pour the 1/4 tblsp kahlua on top.
