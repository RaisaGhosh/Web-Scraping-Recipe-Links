## Ingredients for Burnt Garlic Egg Fried Rice Recipe

  * 50 gram Cooked Rice
  * 1/2  eggs whisked
  * As required A bowl full of chopped and boiled veggies like carrots, broccoli, French beans and green peas
  * 2-2.50  garlic pearls finely chopped
  * 1/4  Big onion finely diced
  * As required A handful of spring onion greens finely chopped
  * 1/4 tablespoon Soya sauce
  * 1/4 tablespoon Vinegar
  * As required Salt
  * 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  * As required A boiled egg to garnish
