How to make Indian Style Chilli ChickenNDTV Food

 **About Indian Style Chilli Chicken Recipe | Chilli Chicken Recipe** : Chilli
chicken is one of the most popular Indo-chinese style chicken appetizer or
starter that is served in Chinese restaurants across the globe. This easy
chilli chicken dish is the sort of snack we need in the winters to warm up on
a cold evening.

**Ingredients of Chilli Chicken Recipe** : Succulent chicken pieces coated in
a thick spicy batter and fried until crisp are added to a garlic-chilli
luscious gravy. Served with noodles or fried rice, the vegetarian alternative
of this dish can be made with paneer, mushroom, potato and tofu.

  * Total Cook Time 50 mins 

  * Prep Time 15 mins 

  * Cook Time 35 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Chilli Chicken**

  * 400 gms boneless chicken
  * 1 1/2 tbsp salt
  * 4 tsp cornflour
  * 2 tsp black pepper
  * 1 egg
  * 3 tsp soy sauce
  * 3 tsp red chilli sauce
  * 8-10 garlic, chopped
  * 8-10 green chillies
  * 2 tsp green chilli sauce
  * 1 tsp vinegar
  * 1 onion, chopped
  * 1 capsicum, chopped
  * 1 tsp black Pepper
  * 1 tsp salt

## How to Make Chilli Chicken

HideShow Media

1.

Take the chicken in a bowl, add salt, cornflour, black pepper, egg, soy sauce
and red chilli sauce.

2.

Mix it well and keep it for marination for about 20 minutes.

3.

After the marination, take out the chicken pieces and deep fry in a pan.

4.

Deep fry the chicken until golden brown.

5.

Now, in another pan put chopped garlic and green chillies. Stir them well.

6.

Add the fried chicken to the pan, followed by soy sauce, green chilli sauce,
vinegar, onion, capsicum, black pepper and salt.

7.

Mix all of these together.

8.

Serve hot.

Key Ingredients: boneless chicken, salt, cornflour, black pepper, egg, soy
sauce, red chilli sauce, garlic, green chillies, green chilli sauce, vinegar,
onion, capsicum, black Pepper, salt
