Boneless Chilli Chicken Recipe







**Boneless Chilli Chicken Recipe**









**Ingredients**  
Chicken (Boneless)  \- 500 gram  
Onion  -1  
Capsicum  -1  
Garlic  -10 cloves  
Vinegar  -2 teaspoon  
Oil  -6 teaspoon  
Egg  -1  
Corn flour  -3 teaspoon  
Salt     As needed  
Black pepper powder  -1/2 teaspoon  
Green Chilli  -8  
Soya sauce  -2 teaspoon  
Red chilli sauce  -2 teaspoon



**Method:**

Take chicken pieces in a bowl, add egg, 1 teaspoon corn flour, salt, pepper
powder and 1 teaspoon soya sauce and mix well.  
Refrigerate it for 3 hours. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the chicken pieces
till golden brown from all sides.  
Heat oil in a pan , add chopped garlic and saute for half a minute. Add green
chilli and saute for another half a minute.  
Add fried chicken pieces in a pan and mix well. Blend the remaining corn flour
in 3 teaspoon of water and mix it in boiled water. Add vinegar and mix well.
cook on medium heat for 2 minutes, Spicy chilli chicken is ready. serve hot
with butter Naan or chapatti.  
Add soya sauce, red chilli sauce, salt and stir once. Add 2 cup of water and
bring it to boil.
