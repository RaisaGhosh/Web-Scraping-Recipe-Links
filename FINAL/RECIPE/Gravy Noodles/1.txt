# About Chicken Gravy Noodles Recipe:

Full meal in one pot. An indo chinese dish. In kolkatta, this s a popular
dish. Noodles with veg & non veg with sauces. We can use lamb or any meat we
like, instead of chicken. Or avoid non veg make it veg gravy, using veg only.
