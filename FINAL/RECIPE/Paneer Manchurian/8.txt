# Restueant Style Paneer Manchurian

×

1 / 1

❮ ❯

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 45

Cook Time : 15

Total Time : 60

Ingredients

Serves 6

  * ingredients for Balls : chopped onion 1/4 cups

  * chopped green chillies  2 tsp 

  * paneer shredded 200 gms

  * chopped Coriander leaves 1 tbsp

  * cornflour 2 tbsp

  * salt 1/2 tsp

  * white papper 1/4 tsp

  * Ingredients for gravy :oil 2 tbsp

  * garlic chopped 2 tsp

  * ginger chopped 1 tsp

  * green chillies chopped 1 tsp

  * chinese salt 1/8 tsp

  * tomato sauce 2 tbsp

  * soya sauce 2 tsp

  * white vinegar 1 tsp

  * white pepper 1/4 tsp

  * salt 3/4 tsp

  * finely chopped green onion 1/4 cups

  * cornflour 2 tsp

  * water 2 tbsp

Directions

  * 01

Method For Making Balls :Mix all these and make small balls. Fry in hot oil.
Keep aside.

  * 02

Methods For Making Gravy :Heat oil and add garlic, ginger, green chillies and
Chinese salt.

  * 03

Mix and saute for 2 mins. Now add Manchurian balls, tomato sauce, soya sauce,
vinegar, salt and white pepper.

  * 04

Cook for 3 mins. Now add green onions and stir fry.

  * 05

Now add cornflour paste and mix well. Cook for 3-4 mins.

  * 06

Serve Hot. ! !
