# Crispy Fried Chicken Wontons Recipe

by TNN

You can't think of celebrations, without thinking about good food as it
happens to be the soul of every party. So, if you too are planning something
special for the weekend, then here's an interesting recipe that you can
prepare at home without putting in much efforts. This exotic recipe might look
very difficult to prepare, but it is certainly the best you thing you can
prepare when in a jiffy. Crispy Fried Chicken Wontons is a delicious Chinese
recipe made using minced chicken, spring onions, eggs and wonton wrappers.
This snack recipe is a must try dish on special occassions. Try this easy
recipe and impress your guests!

## Ingredients of Crispy Fried Chicken Wontons

4 Servings1 Serving2 Servings3 Servings4 Servings5 Servings6 Servings7
Servings8 Servings9 Servings10 Servings11 Servings12 Servings13 Servings14
Servings15 Servings16 Servings17 Servings18 Servings19 Servings20 Servings

  * 300 gm minced chicken
  * 3 minced spring onion leaves
  * 2 tablespoon fish sauce
  * 1 teaspoon powdered spice black pepper
  * 1/2 tablespoon refined oil

  * 2 large minced onion
  * 2 egg whites
  * 2 tablespoon sugar
  * 22 wonton wrapper
  * 1 egg yolk

### How to make Crispy Fried Chicken Wontons

  * #### Step 1

Mix the minced chicken, onions, spring onions, egg white, fish sauce, pepper
powder and sugar in a large bowl.

  * ##### Step 2

Mix the batter well for 4 to 5 minutes or until it gets lightly stiff.

  * ###### Step 3

Separate the wonton wrappers and cut each into triangles and add 1 or 2
tablespoon of the minced chicken mixture on each triangle.

  * ###### Step 4

Brush the edges with the egg yolk and place another triangle on top. Now press
the edges and seal completely.

  * ###### Step 5

Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium flame and fry the prepared wontons
for 2 to 3 minutes on each side or until golden brown and crispy.

  * ###### Step 6

Remove when done and drain the excess oil. Serve hot with ketchup or chilli
sauce.
