# Prawn with lime, chilli and coriander

Add some spice to your barbecue prawns with a fragrant marinade of chilli,
coriander, lime and garlic.

Nov 29, 2014 1:00pm

  * 40 mins preparation
  * 10 mins cooking
  * Serves 4

Print

### Ingredients

Chilli coriander dressing

  * 2 tablespoon peanut oil
  * 2 tablespoon lime juice
  * 1 long red chilli, seeded, chopped finely
  * 1 clove garlic, crushed
  * 2 tablespoon finely chopped fresh coriander
  * sea salt, to taste

Prawn with lime

  * 24 medium uncooked king prawns
  * 2 limes, halved
  * 1 tablespoon peanut oil
  * 1 bunch (350g) watercress (4 cups [110g] trimmed)

### Method

Prawn with lime, chilli and coriander

  * 1

To make chilli coriander dressing, whisk all ingredients in a small bowl until
combined.

  * 2

Remove heads and legs from prawns, leaving tail and back shell intact. Split
the prawns along the underside to open out flat, leaving shells on.

  * 3

Cook lime halves on a heated, oiled grill plate (or grill or barbecue) until
browned lightly; remove. Brush prawns with oil; cook prawns on same grill
plate until browned and cooked through.

  * 4

Serve prawns with watercress. Drizzle with chilli coriander dressing and serve
with limes.
