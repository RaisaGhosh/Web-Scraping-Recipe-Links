# Spicy Sriracha Prawns with Basil Recipe

  * Food Blogger Divya Burman 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How to make Spicy Sriracha Prawns with Basil

 **About Spicy Sriracha Prawns with Basil Recipe** : Make this yummy dish of
prawns within minutes. Prawns tossed with spicy sauces, chillies and basil is
all what you need as your evening snacks.

  * Total Cook Time 30 mins 

  * Prep Time 10 mins 

  * Cook Time 20 mins 

  * Recipe Servings4

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Spicy Sriracha Prawns with Basil**

  * 10-12 Medium prawns (deveined and deshelled)
  * 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  * 1 red and green chillies, chopped
  * 3 tsp oyster sauce
  * 2 tsp sriracha chilli sauce
  * 1 tsp fish sauce
  * Lemon juice
  * 15 basil leaves
  * 3 spring onion greens

## How to Make Spicy Sriracha Prawns with Basil

HideShow Media

1.

Heat oil in a wok and fry garlic and chillies.

2.

Add prawns and stir fry.

3.

Add oyster sauce, fish sauce and sriracha.

4.

Cook till sauce coats prawns. If too dry add little water. Add basil leaves.

5.

Squeeze lemon juice and serve with spring onion greens.

Key Ingredients: prawns (deveined and deshelled), garlic cloves, red and green
chillies, oyster sauce, sriracha chilli sauce, fish sauce, Lemon juice, basil
leaves, spring onion greens
