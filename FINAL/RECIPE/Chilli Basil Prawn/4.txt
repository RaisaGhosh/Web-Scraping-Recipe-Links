# Chilli basil prawn stir-fry with noodles

#### 11 Ingredients

  * 400g wok-ready Singapore-style noodles 

  * 2 tbsp peanut oil 

  * 500g peeled prawns, tails intact 

  * 1 red onion, cut into wedges 

  * 200g snow peas, trimmed 

  * 2 long red chillies, thinly sliced diagonally 

  * 2 tbsp sweet chilli sauce 

  * 2 tsp fish sauce 

  * 2 tsp lime juice 

  * 20g punnet basil, leaves picked 

  * Sliced red chilli, extra, to serve 

  * Select all ingredients 

Add to shopping list

#### 3 Method Steps

  * Step 1

Place noodles in a bowl and cover with boiling water. Set aside for 1 min.
Separate noodles with a fork. Drain.

  * Step 2

Meanwhile, heat 1 tablespoon oil in a wok over high heat. Add prawns and cook
for 1 min or until just changed colour. Transfer to a bowl.

  * Step 3

Heat remaining oil in the wok. Cook onion and snow peas for 2 mins or until
onion is starting to brown. Add chilli, sweet chilli sauce, fish sauce, lime
juice, prawns and noodles and stir-fry for 2 mins or until heated through.
Stir in basil. Serve topped with extra chilli.
