# Fish in hot garlic sauce

March 20, 2012March 20, 2012 sarwatabbasi

  

**Ingredients for marination:**

  1. Fish (fillet) 1/2 kg.
  2. Salt 1/2 tsp.
  3. Black pepper 1/2 tsp.
  4. Soy sauce 1 tbsp.
  5. Vinegar 1 tbsp.

**Ingredients for batter:**

  1. Egg 1
  2. Corn flour 2 tbsp.
  3. Flour 2 tbsp.
  4. Salt a pinch
  5. Baking powder 1/2 tsp.
  6. Oil for deep frying

**Ingredients for sauce:**

  1. Green chillies (chopped) 8
  2. Garlic (chopped) 8 cloves
  3. Soy sauce 1 tbsp.
  4. Chilli sauce 1 tsp.
  5. Corn flour 1 tbsp.
  6. Chicken powder 1/2 tsp.
  7. Chicken stock or water 1 cup
  8. Oil 1 tbsp.

**Method:**

  * Marinade the fish with salt, soy sauce, black pepper and vineger for half an hour.
  * In a bowl take egg, salt, corn flour, flour and baking powder.
  * Mix them together to form a batter (add little cold water if required).
  * Dip fish in the batter and deep fry till golden brown.
  * **For sauce:** Heat oil in a pan.
  * Add green chillies and garlic.
  * Fry and add chicken stock or water.
  * Bring to boil.
  * Add soy sauce and chicken powder.
  * Dissolve corn flour in little water.
  * Add it to the boiling stock and cook till the sauce gets thick.
  * Place the fried fish in a serving dish and pour sauce over it.
  * Serve with rice.
