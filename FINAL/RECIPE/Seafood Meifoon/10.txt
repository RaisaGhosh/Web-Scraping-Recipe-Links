Chow mei fun / Chow rice vermicelli(炒米粉) are quite popular in South East Asia,
particularly in Indonesia, Malaysia and Singapore.

Ingredients

  * 200 g Mei Fun(dry rice noodles)

  * 6 fish cakes (thinly cut)
