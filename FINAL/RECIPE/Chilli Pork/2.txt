# Roasted Chilli Pork Recipe

  * Ram Bahadur Budhathoki 

  * Chowman 

  * Review 

  * Overview
  * Cook Time
  * Ingredients
  * Method

How To Make Roasted Chilli Pork

 **About Roasted Chilli Pork Recipe** : Marinated pork double fried and tossed
in hoisin sauce, tomato sauce, chilli sauce and black bean sauce with a
squeeze of lime. Aren't you slurping already?

  * Total Cook Time 40 mins 

  * Prep Time 15 mins 

  * Cook Time 25 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Medium

###  **Ingredients of Roasted Chilli Pork**

  * Onion, green capsicum, cabbage cut into diced pieces
  * slice roasted pork
  * ginger, onion, celery., chopped
  * Black bean
  * Chilli Paste
  * Tomato ketchup
  * Hoisin sauce
  * Dark soya sauce
  * Lemon juice
  * Wine
  * Salt , pepper, sugar

## How to Make Roasted Chilli Pork

HideShow Media

1.

Take 1 tea spoon oil with chopped ginger onion, celery, saute for a few
seconds.

2.

Add onion, green capsicum, cabbage.

3.

Add 1 tea spoon black bean sauce.

4.

Add 2 tea spoon chilli paste, 1 tea spoon tomato ketchup. 1 tea spoon hoisin
sauce.

5.

Take half tea spoon dark soya sauce, salt pepper, sugar as per taste.

6.

Finish with lemon juice 1 tea spoon, wine half tea spoon.

7.

Garnish with coriander leaf and serve.

Key Ingredients: Onion, green capsicum, cabbage cut into diced pieces, slice
roasted pork, ginger, onion, celery., Black bean, Chilli Paste, Tomato
ketchup, Hoisin sauce, Dark soya sauce, Lemon juice, Wine, Salt , pepper,
sugar
