### Ingredients

  * 1 kg - pork. 
  * 3 tsps - turmeric. 
  * Salt. 
  * 4 tbsp - tomato sauce. 
  * 4 tbsp - green chilli sauce. 
  * 2 capsicums, cubed and fried| To mince: 
  * 2 - onions. 
  * 12 - green chillies. 
  * 1 - tomato. 
  * 1 inch - ginger. 
  * 2 pods - garlic. 

# How to Make Pork chilli

  * Marinate the pork with turmeric and salt for 15 mins. 
  * Then fry the pork pieces and keep aside. 
  * Fry the minced ingredients well. 
  * Then add tomato sauce and green chilli sauce. Add salt if required. 
  * Garnish with 2 fried capsicums.
