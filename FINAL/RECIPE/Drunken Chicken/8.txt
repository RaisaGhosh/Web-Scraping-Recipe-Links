## Ingredients for Drunken And Chicken Recipe

  * 1.50 piece Chicken breasts cut in small
  * 1/2 tablespoon Emerils essence
  * 2.50  Cloves of garlic minced
  * 1/2 tablespoon Butter
  * 1/2 tablespoon Flour
  * 1/4 cup White wine
  * 0.13 cup Bourbon
  * 1/2 cup 1/2 And 1/2 Cream
  * As required Parsley For Garnish
