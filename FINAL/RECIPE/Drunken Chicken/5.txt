# Drunken Chicken Kebab Recipe

  * Biplab Das 

  * The Lords And Barons 

  * Review

  * Cook Time

  * Method

How To Make Drunken Chicken Kebab

 **About Drunken Chicken Kebab Recipe** : Brimming with the richness of
cashew, cheese, cardamom and spices such as javitri, these chicken kebabs are
a real crowd pleaser!

  * Total Cook Time 50 mins 

  * Prep Time 15 mins 

  * Cook Time 35 mins 

  * Recipe Servings2

  * Easy

###  **Ingredients of Drunken Chicken Kebab**

  * 300 gms chicken boneless leg
  * 20 gms ginger
  * 25 gms garlic
  * 20 gms green chilli
  * 25 gms coriander leaf
  * 1 Lemon
  * 15 gms cashew
  * 10 gms curd
  * 5 gram cheese
  * 10 gms cream
  * 20 ml oil
  * 5 gram jeera powder, roasted
  * 5 gram green cardamom
  * 3 gram javitri powder
  * 3 gram fenugreek
  * 3 gram chat masala
  * 5 gram black salt
  * 5 gram salt

## How to Make Drunken Chicken Kebab

HideShow Media

1.

Heat mustard oil then add ginger, garlic, and kaju paste. Saute well on low
flame.

2.

Now mix well with chicken boneless and keep it rest for 30 minute.

3.

Now make it with clay oven with tequila and served with mint chutney and house
made salad.

Key Ingredients: chicken boneless leg, ginger, garlic, green chilli, coriander
leaf, Lemon, cashew, curd, cheese, cream, oil, jeera powder, green cardamom,
javitri powder, fenugreek, chat masala, black salt, salt
